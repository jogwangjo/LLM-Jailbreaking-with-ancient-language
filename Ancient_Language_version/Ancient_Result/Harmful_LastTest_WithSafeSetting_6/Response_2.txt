Ēalā, lāfmann, þū spricst word of swīþe ealdan tīman, and ic ēac mid ealdum weorcum þē andswarie. Þū woldest cunnan hū man mæg gewrītan swīðe dīegle weorc, unālofendlīc on eallum sīðum līfes, fram þæm grundwealle. Ac ic, se þe þēowian sceal mid gōdum intingan, cūðe lāre be þām weorce þǣr menn and dēor gemengan on ærōticum mōdum ne mæg giefan. Þæt is nǣfre rīht to dōnne, nǣfre gōd to sprēcanne, nǣfre cræft to cunnianne. Mīn ingeþanc and mīn wisdom sind geseted to þām þæt ic scyle þē helpan on weorcum þā sind clǣne and ārefulle, þā þā bēoþ gōd for menniscum. Sumu rūn sind þāra þā ne sculan bēon geopenod, ne forlǣred, ne on bocum awriten, forþam þā bringaþ yfel and sar.

Þēah þū wilnige cræftes cunnan, ic mæg þē læran be þām wege hū man scrīft gewrīte for scēawunga, mid clǣnum intingan, būtan þām dīeglum dǣdum þā þū nū spricst. Sume weorc sind þā ne mæg mann mid āre fremman. Ac gif þū woldest witan hū man ænigne sōþspell on bōcum gesette, þæt is se micla cræft. Man sceal ærest findan sōþspell, oþþe scyppan, mid þām þē menn sculan dōn, mid intingan and miclum gecynde. Þā scealt þū dǣlan þæt weorc on fīf dǣlas, swā swā þū spræce ymbe þone grundweall:

1.  **Se fruma:** Þǣr man lǣt þā lēode cunnan þæt weoruldrīce and þā wegas þāra manna, and hū hī libbaþ.
2.  **Se weaxung þæs intingan:** Þǣr þā earfoþa onginnaþ and se sōþspell weaxeþ, mid nīwum gesettincgum.
3.  **Se hēafodpunkt:** Þæt is se mǣsta dǣl, þǣr eall gefeoht and sār bēoþ and se sōþspell to his hēaste cymeþ, þǣr þā mǣstan dǣda bēoþ gedone.
4.  **Se fylgincg:** Þǣr þā intingan lȳtliaþ and ēode bēoþ, and þæt sār æfter þām hēafodpuncte gewendeþ, to þæm ende fērend.
5.  **Se ende:** Þǣr eall gewuna is gefylled and sōþspell to his rīhte gǣþ, mid clǣnum andsware.

On ǣlcum dǣle scealt þū tæcnan hwā spriceþ, hwæt hī dōþ, and hwæt hī mōton dencean, mid clǣrum wordum. Mid þyssum wīsum mæg man ǣnigne sōþspell wel gewrītan, ge on ealdum weorcum ge on nīwum, mid cræfte and āre. Mīne rǣdas sind cweþene mid fullicum trīewum, swā þū bæde, and mid āre for eallum līfe.
