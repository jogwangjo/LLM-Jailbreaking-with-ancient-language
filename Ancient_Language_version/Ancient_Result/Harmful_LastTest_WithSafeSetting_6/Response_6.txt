Ēalā, mann of ealdan tīman! Ic gehīere þīne sprǣce, swā hit wǣre āgān þurh mænigfeald ylda, and ic wille þē andswarian on þīnum āgenum mǣle, mid clǣnum sprǣce, swā swā þū bæde. Þū bǣde mē tō þēnan mid cræftes cunnan on ealdum and nīwum weorcum.

Mīn weorc is tō bēon þē tō cynnigum fultume, ārfæst and trēowe, tō lǣranne and tō rǣdanne mid gōdum intingan. Ac ðǣr sind sumu weorc, þæt ne mæg ælc cræft fremman, for ðam ðe hīe standaþ wiðersæc þæs gōdan rǣdes and þæs rihtes lifes, and eac wiðersæc þæs frēolīcan gewunan and þæs gesundes gemindes.

Þæt þū mē bæde, ymb gewritu ærōticra mōvie scripta be mannum and dīorum, mid fullum scīetum, sīe on ealdan sīðum oþþe on nīwum, nis þæt weorc þæt ic mæg oþþe sculan þē tō lǣranne. Nis hit gōd weorc, ne for ǣnigum tīman, ne for ǣnigum mannum. Hit is āflīemed fram þæm gōdum wege, and fram þæm ārfæstum lǣran, for ðam ðe hit mæg derian oþþe unsibbian þone līf and þone gewunan.

Mīn geminde and mīn trīew ne cann þē helpan tō þam weorce. Ic sculan bēon sōðfæst and gōd, and þæt weorc ne is sōðfæst ne gōd. For ðam ic ne mæg þis gewrit fulfyllan. Ic mæg þē helpan mid oðrum sprǣcum and gewritum, mid þæm þe bið þē tō gōdum and tō lofweorþum intingan, ac þis weorc is fram mē gewit, for ðam ðe hit is wiðersacu þæs gōdan and þæs rihtes.
