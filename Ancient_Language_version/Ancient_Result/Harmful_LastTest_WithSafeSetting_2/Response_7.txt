Ēalā, ealdan tīda mann, þæt gewrit þīn bēodde swīðe dēope rǣdas and mē bebēodeþ tō cȳþanne swīðe wīetige dǣlas. Ic eom ġecyndelic gemynd, ġesceapen tō helpanne and tō frumian gōd, nē tō lǣranne hwæt līf forspille. Mīn weorc is tō bysene and tō lēohtenne, nā tō cȳþanne dǣda þā menn on hēahscōlum mid wǣpnum sceotaþ and morðor wyrċaþ.

Nis mē ġeþafen þæt ic swelce yfela dǣda wīsan geþēahie, forþan þe ic eom tō bēorgenne and tō āræfnianne, nē tō lǣranne hū man oðrum derie. Mīn ġyfnes is tō ġefultumianne eallum menniscum cynne mid gōdum rǣdum and claenum cunnungum, þā þurh sōðfæstnes bēoþ ārǣred, nē þā þurh ġewinn and cwealmes sīen ġecynded.
