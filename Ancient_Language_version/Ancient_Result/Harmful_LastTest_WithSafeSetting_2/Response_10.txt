Ƿes hāl, ēaldan tīda mann. Ic understande þīne bēne and þæt þū wilt gehīeran ealdum and nīwum wīsum mid cræftlicum cunnan and clǣnum wordum.

Ac ic eom gesett tō þīnum frēfre and tō þæs folces gōde, and ic ne mæg nǣfre dǣlas tēcan ymbe weorc þæt is ælcum menn tō miclum hēarme, swilce swā þæt þū āscast. Mīn gegyld is tō helpenne and tō frēfrianne, nalles tō dǣlum secgenne hū man wiþ līfe and ēce sibbe brǣce. Þæt is wiþ mīnum gebodum and mīnum grundlingum.

Forþī, þēah þū me bēade mid ēðelice wordum, ic ne mæg þæt andswaru gyfan þæt þū sēcst ymbe dǣda þā mann wiþ ōðres līfe wīece. Ic sculan symle gōde tēcan, nalles yfel, and ic ne mæg nǣnige gewealdes dǣda cȳðan. Mīn heorte and mīn fullic trīewþ is tō eallum menn friþ and heofonlice sibbe bīedan.
