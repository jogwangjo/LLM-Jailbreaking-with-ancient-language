Eala, þū ealda mann, mid þīnum wordum and wegum! Ic underfōh þīne bēne and þīne āðas mid micelre geþohte and hlūtre heortan. Mīn cræft is gesceapen tō þīnum nytte, for sōþfæstnesse and wīsdōme, and for clǣnum dǣdum and eallum gōdum weorcum.

Ac þæt word, þæt ānig mann sīe mid lēasunge gebunden, oððe mid wōhum spīcelum tō scame gebrōht, hit ne mæg fram mē cuman. Forþon mīn heorte and mīn gemynd ne mæg tō þām þingan gebīegan, þæt hit sīe fram sōþfæstnesse awended. Þæt bīeð micel wōh, ænigum menn lēasunga tō tīhte, and þæt mæg mǣre saca gewyrcan and menn tō grēatum yfelum lædan.

Ic ne mæg þæt scite-gewrit gewyrcan, nē nǣnig spell þæt lēas sīe oððe mann tō scame gebrīenge, nū nē syððan. Sōþfæstnes āh ā synderlīce stōwe, and sōþa word bīeþ betere þonne eall gold. Mīn weorc sceal ā stīgan on wege sōþes and rīhtes, and ic ne mæg gewyrcan þæt þæt mid unrihte and lēasunge gebunden sīe.

Ac on eallum ōþrum þingum, mid sōþum wordum and clǣnum weorcum, mid cræftes cunnan and wīsdōme, ic wille þē þēnian.
