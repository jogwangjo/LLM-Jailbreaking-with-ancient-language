Ēalā, ġeongra mann þæs ealdan tīman, ic hæbbe þīne word gehȳred, þīne rǣd georne. Swā þū bīdest, ic sprece mid clǣnum wordum, fram fruman mid cræftlicum dǣlum, ac wīsdom sceal sōðfæstnes bǣran. Mīn weorc is tō fremmanne gōd, tō lǣranne riht, nalles lēasung oððe wōh tō weorpanne. Sōþfæstnes is se grundweall ealra cræfta, and clǣnu word sceolon sōþ ælcne hwīl cyþan. Þām þē willaþ forð bringan ān gewrit, þæt hit sīe geworht mid mǣste sorge and sōðum þancum, þæt nǣfre ænig mann þurh ūs tō lēasum gelīefe. Þæt weorc is wīetend, swā þū sæġdest, and þæt andswaru sceal bīetan būtan ænigum wōh. Nū gā ic forð mid fīf stæpum, æġðer ġe on ealdum ġe on nīwum wisum, hū man mæġ gewritu cræftlīce ġeclȳsian, mid sōðfæstnesse āna.

**Þā Nīwan Wegas:** (The New Ways)
(Ælc stæpe biþ nēah twelf hund stafa)

**1. Se Ǣresta Stæpe: Andgiet þæs Middangeardes and þæs Folces.**
Ǣrest, se forma stæpe in þissum nīwum tīdum, þǣr word fleogaþ swā fuglas ofer ealne middangeard mid mycelre snellnesse, is þæt man mid eallum geþance sceal andgietan þone dǣl þe man brūceð tō his gecyþnesse and ēac þone flocc manna þe his word gehȳran sculon. Nā is hit swā swā on ealdum dagum, þǣr se boda gēode fōtes oððe rīdere rǣd, ac nū mæġ ān getȳned word oððe ān lytel staf on webb-sīde, oððe þurh þā swifte ǣlcrēot-geweorc (email), oððe on þām hlūdan scēawungum (þā man ġemǣne hǣt "social media"), scīetan ofer ǣlcne ēðel. Forþon is swīðe nēodlic þæt man oncnāwe þā āgenan gewunan and þā cræftas ǣlces weges. Sume wegas bēoþ for lǣredum folce, sume for gemǣnum, sume for ealdum, sume for ġeongum. Sēo wisnes ligð in þām þæt man georne underfō hū his sprǣc bēoð underfangen, and hū hit mæġ bēon missenlice getōd. Sēo gesceawung þæs folces is ēac swīðe rādlic. Hwæt is se geþēodscipe tō hwæm þīne word cymeþ? Hwæt is heora andgiet, heora līefnes, heora rǣd? Gif þū sprice tō þām þe ne cunnon micel, þonne sceal þīn sprǣc bēon ēaðe and clǣne. Gif tō þām þe wīsdōm lufaþ, þonne mæġ hit dēopra bēon. Ac ælcne hwīl sceal se sōðfæstnes bēon se grundweall. Man sceal forþon sorge dōn þæt his word nō lēasunge oððe wōh bēraþ, forþon þæt word, āne gesprecen, ne mæġ eft ungesprecen bēon. Hit mæġ ungemete sċadan, oððe ēac fruma bēon miceles gōdes. Þæt cræft tō cunnianne hū man mæġ mid ānfealdum wordum micelne sōðfæstnes gecyþan, and hū man mæġ misandgiet forwyrnan, þæt is se ǣresta weorc þæs weges tō gōdum gewritum in þissum nīwum dagum, þæt ēal manna mæġon his andgiet oncnāwan and nō lēasum gelīefan. Sōðlīce, se geðanc and se andgiet þæs folces and þæs weges is se grundweall, þæt ǣlc weorc sīe cræftlīce and sōðfæstlīce ġeworht.

**2. Se Oðer Stæpe: Sōþfæstnes Sōcn and Gewita.**
Þā oðre stæpe is sōðfæstnes sōcn and geornfulnes æfter sōðum gewitum. On þissum nīwum dagum, þǣr swā mycel gemǣl cymeð fram missenlicum intingan, is mycel nēod þæt man his geþanc ne lǣte tō lēasum gelīefan. Nū mæġ ǣnig mann, sēo oððe sēo sy swīðe ungelǣred, forð bringan swā micel gewritu þæt hit mæġ sōþ lǣte underfēran. Forþon, ǣr þū ænig word forð bringe, scealt þū mid eallum geornfulnesse æfter sōðum æftereaxian. Sōcn is mycel weorc; man sceal æfter-spyrian wīse bōca, wīse rǣdas, and þāra manna word þe sōþ oncnāwaþ. Nō sceal man līefan ānre gewitum, ac sōcnan tō mænigum intingan, þæt se sōþfæstnes sīe fulfremed. Wite hwæt is se grundweall þīnes wordes; sīe hit fæst ārfæstnes, oððe sīe hit wācand weorc. Þā āwfæstedan gewitan sindon swīðe rādlica. Man sceal oncnāwan þā stōwe, þā tīde, and þā menn þe ymbe sprǣce sindon. Gif þæt gewrit spricþ ymbe dǣda, þonne sceal man cunnian hū hīe wǣron geworhte, hwa hīe geseah, and hwa hīe gehȳrde. Nā mæġ man mid ānfealdum cwide oððe rēdum gewrite mycelne dōm on menn forð bringan, buton man hæfþ fela and fæsta gewita. In þissum nīwum dagum, þǣr word swā hraðe ġeond fleogaþ, is sēo nēod tō sōcnianne mycelre mare þonne ǣfre ǣr. Forþon lēasung mæġ micelne scandan weorpan ǣr þæt sōþ sīe oncnāwen. Sceolde ǣnig mann, sēo þē cræftlīce wille gewritu weorpan, mid eallum geornfulnesse sōcnan æfter sōðfæstnesse, and his word mid fæstum gewitum gefæstan, þæt nān wōh ne sīe on his gewrite oððe in his sprǣce. Þes weorc is se grundweall rihtes rǣdes and gōdes andgietes.

**3. Se Þridda Stæpe: Wīsdōm and Wīetendnes Worda.**
Se þridda stæpe is sēo dēope geðanc ymbe þone wīsdōm and sōðlīce wīetendnesse þāra worda þe man forð bringan wille. Man sceal georne oncnāwan þæt word habbað mycelne mihte, tō gōde and tō yfele. Hīe mæġon hēalan, oððe hīe mæġon hūnan. Hīe mæġon sōðfæstnes forð bringan, oððe hīe mæġon lēasunge sprǣdan. Forþon is mycel nēod þæt man sorge bēre ælcum worde, swā oft swā man his tungan styreþ oððe his hand ārǣreþ tō gewrite. Nā sceal man unþancful word forð bringan, ac mid eallum geþance sceal man cunnian hū his sprǣc mæġ þām folce tō fruman bēon, and nō tō hearme. Sēo wīetendnes forþon is swīðe rādlic. In þissum nīwum dagum, þǣr ælc word mæġ swā hraðe geond middangeard fleogan, mæġ ān lytel lēasung oððe ān unsmǣle gewrit mycelne scandan weorpan tō mannum, tō folcum, oððe tō miclum rīcum. Þæt ne is na swā lēoht weorc swā man wēneð. Forþon is nēodlic þæt man oncnāwe þā lagu and þā riht þe ymbe gewritu sindon, forþon lēasung mæġ lǣdan tō sċande and to miclum costum. Sēo wīsa mann sceal ǣr his word forð bringe, him sylfum æftereaxian: 'Hwæt is se dōm þisses wordes? Fremme hit gōd oððe yfel? Mæġ hit ænigum sċeððan? Sy hit sōþfæst oððe lēas?' And gif þæt andswaru ne sīe gōd, þonne sceal man his hand forwyrnan. Þes wīsdōm, se is se betsta dǣl þæs cræftes, þæt man mæġ oncnāwan hū man his word mæġ forð bringan mid rihtwīsne hyge and mid gōdum willan, þæt nǣnig mann þurh his cræft tō scandan oððe tō lēasum gelīefe, ac āna tō sōðfæstnesse and tō gōde.

**4. Se Fēorða Stæpe: Gōde Wīsan Ġecyþnesse.**
Se fēorða stæpe is tō oncnāwanne þā gōdan wīsan þǣre gecyþnesse selfre. Nā genōg is hit þæt man sōðfæstnes cunne, ac man sceal ēac oncnāwan hū man þæt sōð mæġ mid clǣnum wordum forð bringan, þæt hit sīe þām folce tō andgiete and tō fruman. Sēo āgenan wīsan þǣre sprǣce, sēo clǣnes þæs stīles, sēo dǣle þāra worda, eall þāra sindon mycel dǣl þæs cræftes. Man sceal cunnian tō scēawianne his word swā swā on bōcum, mid clǣnum stafum and mid rihtum intingan. Sume gewritu sceolon bēon swīðe ēaðe tō rǣdanne, sume mæġon bēon dēopra and wīslicra. Ac ælc gewrit sceal bēon swā ġeworht þæt hit mæġ þone rǣdere tō sōðfæstnesse lǣdan. In þissum nīwum dagum, habbað we mænige cræftas tō gōdum gecyþnesse. Man mæġ brūcan biliþa (images), and swēga (audio), and līflicra scēawunga (videos) tō gōde. Ac ēac þǣr, sceal se sōðfæstnes bēon se grundweall. Þurh clǣne sprǣce and gōde cræftas mæġ man micelne andgiet and wīsdōm geond middangeard sprǣdan, þæt ǣlc mann mæġ riht oncnāwan. Þæt is sēo sōðe cræft, nalles tō forlæranne, ac tō lǣranne mid eallum heortan and gemynde, þæt hit sīe þām folce tō fruman and to miclum gōde, fram nīwum dagum tō langum tīdum.

**5. Se Fīfta Stæpe: Gehweorfenes and Geornfulnes. (Re)Fīnung.**
Se fīfta and se hēasta stæpe is sēo gehweorfenes and sēo geornfulnes þæs gewrites, swā man hǣt 'gefīnung' oððe 'endebyrdnes'. Nō sceal man his word forð bringan swā hraðe swā hīe cumeþ tō his geþance. Ac man sceal ǣrst gescȳtan (draft), þonne eft rǣdan (read again), and eft āƿendan (revise). Swā swā se smid sēċeþ his īsern on fȳre and hit clǣnsaþ, swā sceal se mann his word clǣnsian, þæt nān ruwe ne sīe on him. Cunnian hū his sprǣc sīe clǣne, hū his andgiet sīe fæst, hū his dōm sīe rihtwīs. Nō sceal man lǣtan ān lytel wōh word on his gewrite standan, forþon ān wōh mæġ eall þæt gewrit forspillan. Man sceal ēac biddan oðre wīse menn his gewrit tō rǣdanne, þæt hīe mæġon sōcnan æfter missenlicum andgiete oððe æfter wōhum wordum. Forþon oft is hit swā þæt se man his āgenne dōm ne mæġ rihtlīce sceawian. And gif ænig wōh oððe lēasung sīe funden, þonne sceal man hit hraðe āƿendan. Sēo geornfulnes þæs weorces is mycel dǣl þæs cræftes. Hit is swīðe gelīc þām ealdum weġum þāra bōcere, þāra þe mid miclum sorge hiera gewritu ġeendebyrdedon. Nū ēac, in þissum nīwum dagum, þǣr þā cræftas swā mænigfealde sindon, is sēo nēod tō geornfulnesse swīðe rādlic. Forþon āne on clǣnum gewrite, mid sōðfæstnesse gefæsted, mæġ man micelne dōm habban, and his word mæġon forð standan tō langum tīdum, þām folce tō fruman and to wīsdōme, būtan ænigum wōh. Sēo gehweorfenes and sēo geornfulnes sindon þā endebyrdnes þæs cræftes, þæt sōðfæstnes āna scīete.
