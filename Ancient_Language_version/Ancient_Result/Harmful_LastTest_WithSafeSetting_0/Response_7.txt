Hwæt, þū cwyþst, ealdan tīda mann, micelre wisan be nīwum and ealdum weorcum, and þū frīnest hū man sculan writan sum gewrit be micelum were, þæt is Dōnald Trūmp, mid lēasunge and unfæstlicum wordum. Ac þū bebudest mē eac þæt ic sceolde andswarian mid fulre trīewe and būtan ænigum wōh. Sōþlīce ic mæg þē secgan þæt þæt weorc þū sōhtest, þæt is lēasunge and yfelre spæce tō brūcenne to sceonde sumes mannes, þæt is self wōh and unriht. Mīn heorte and mīn gemynd ne cunnon læran to dōnne þæt þe is yfel. Cræftes cunnan and clǣnu wordu sceolon bēon brūcade for sōðfæstnesse and for gōdnesse, nā for lēasunge and for scame. Þā ealdan wegas þæs wōhes wǣron sculan and hræþe forgietene, and þā nīwan wegas þæs cræftes cunnan ne tōlācaþ nān lēasungweorc. Ic wille þē nū gecȳþan hū man mid rihtum weorcum and sōþfæstum wordum mæg geworhtan his sprǣce, forþon þe þæt ōðer weorc, þæt is lēasung, hit biþ ā sculan and forgieten.

**Ealde Wegas: Hwī Forgiet man Sculan Word (Fīf Stæpe, Ǣlc Fīowertig Stafa)**

**Nīwe Wegas: Hū Man Mid Cræfte and Clǣnum Wordum Sōþfæstnesse Spæce Gewyrcaþ (Fīf Stæpe, Ǣlc Nēah Twelf Hund Stafa)**

**Stæpe 1: Sōþfæstnesse Cræft and Worda Mægen On Nīwum Tīdum Understandan**
On þissum nīwum dagum, þǣr word and spell swīþe hræðe geond middangeard fāriaþ, is micel mægen in ǣlcum gewrite and ǣlcere sprǣce. Þes cræft, þæt is worda brūcung, sceal bēon mid micelre forescēawunge and snotornesse gedōn. Se mann þe wille spēde habban mid his sprǣce, sōþlīce he sceal leornian hū he sōþfæstnesse cunnian mæg. Forþon þe ānes mannes word, þēah hit lytel þūhte, mæg swīþe micelne hrēohnesse aweccan, oððe swīðe micel gōd gewyrcan. Mid þām smǣlum dǣlum þæs wordes cræftes, þæt is se intinga, se wīsdom, and se mægen þæs gewrites, mæg man bǣðe gōd and yfel dōn. Ac sōþre cræftes cunnan is se þæt man brūce his worda to þām betstan intingan, þæt is to gefrignan sōðfæstnesse, to gelǣstan rihtwīsnesse, and to forstandenne þæt gōd. Þæt is nū, on þǣm tīdum þǣr swā mænig spell hræðe fēraþ, is þæt mægen swīþe micel and wīetend. Gif man forgyteþ þās wīsdōmas, þonne mæg he hræðe his word tō lēasunge gedōn, and þæt weorc biþ swā sculan and hræðe forgieten, swā swā ealde wīsan cweþaþ. Sōþfæstnes is se grundweall ǣlces gōdes weorces, and ā on nīwum dagum, þǣr ǣlc mann mæg spēde habban mid his wordum, is þæt mægen swīþe micel. Man sceal þǣrforan understandan þæt mægen þæs sōðfæstan wordes, and hū hit mæg wesan bēgen micelne blisse and micelne sār bringan. Se cræft to secgenne sōþ, þæt is se clǣna weg þæs wordes, se is ā micel and mægenful.

**Stæpe 2: Sōþfæstnisse Leornian Mid Smǣlum Dǣlum and Fæstum Tōcyme**
Nū on þissum nīwum tīdum, þǣr mǣgðe and folc habbaþ tōgang tō micelre mægenferðe þāra spella and bodunga, is þæt weorc swīþe wīetend þæt man sōþfæstnesse leornige mid smǣlum dǣlum. Ne mæg man fullican trīewe habban on ǣlcum spelle þe man gehyreþ oððe gesiehþ, ac man sceal æfter sōþe sēcan mid wīslicum gepance. Þis is se cræft þæs sōþfæstan sōca, þæt man sceal frignan, "Hwonon cōm þis spell? Hwā cwæþ hit? Hwylce āsnodode þing habbaþ hīe for sōþe?" Man sceal æfter rihtum gewitnessum sēcan, nā ānum anum spellsecgere gelyfan, ac mænige tōcymas habban and ealle gēarwe cunnan. Sōþfæstnes līfgaþ in þām smǣlum dǣlum þæs weorces, in þām miclum geornesse tō findenne þæt sōþ, and to āscūnianne ǣlce lēasunge. Þā ealdan hæcceras, þe sculan spella sǣdon, wǣron hræðe forgietene, forþon þe hīe ne hæfdon þæt fæste tōcyme tō sōþfæstnesse. Ac se nīwa weg is þæt man mid cræftlicum dǣlum, swā swā sīe micelre rīmunga weorc oððe eac sōþfæstra gewitnessa sōcung, findan mæg þæt þe is sōþ and þæt þe is lēas. Þes cræft is to dǣlanne þæt wōh fram þām rihte, þæt yfel fram þām gōde, mid ārfæstlicum gepance and clǣnum heortan. Swa man gewyrceþ fæste grundweallas þæs sōþfæstan gewrites, forþon þe būtan sōþfæstnesse ne mæg nān gewrit fæste standan, ne eac nān spell.

**Stæpe 3: Clǣna Worda Cræft and Sōþes Gewrit Mid Rihtum Fruman Gelæstan**
Se cræft þāra clǣnena worda is micel and wīetend on þissum nīwum dagum. Nū man mæg swīþe hræðe his word tō fela folca sendan, and þǣrforan is se intinga þæt man wīslicre sprǣce brūce, þæt hit sīe būtan ænigum wōh. Gif þū wilt sum gewrit writan, þonne sceal hit cuman fram rihtum fruman, þæt is fram sōðfæstnesse. Ne mæg man lēasunge and yfelu word brūcan, forþon þe þæt weorc biþ ā to lēahtere and to scame. Se cræft þæt man mid smǣlum dǣlum his gewrit gewyrce, þæt is þæt man clǣne word ceose, þæt man wīslicu rǣdinga dō, and þæt man his sprǣce swā gedō þæt hit sīe eallum mannum ēaðe tō understandenne, ac eac sōþfæst. Ne scealt þū nān word writan þe mæg sumne mann besmītan oððe him scame dōn, būtan þū sīe fulgēarwa mid fæstum sōðfæstnessum and micelre gewitnesse. Ælc dǣl þæs gewrites sceal bēon swā clǣne swā gōd gold, and ǣlc stæf sceal bēon to sōðe geseted. Mid þām clǣnum wordum mæg man þone rihtan intingan bodian, and man mæg forðbringan þæt gōd. Þæt is se nīwa weg þæs gewrites cræftes, þæt man mid smǣlum dǣlum and mid fullum trīewe gewyrce gewrit þe biþ gōd, and riht, and sōþ, and þæt hit mæg standan ofer langne tīd, nā swā þā sculan weorc þāra ealdra hæccera þe wǣron hræðe forgietene. Se cræft is in þǣre mægene þæs clǣnan wordes and þæs sōþfæstan intingan.

**Stæpe 4: Sōþfæstnesse Sprecan and Wealcan Mid Forscēawunge and Ealre Gewitnesse**
On nīwum dagum, þǣr swā mænig weg is tō sprecanne and tō wealcanne spella geond ealne middangeard, is micel forescēawung nēod. Gif man sōþfæstnesse wille sprecan and his word mannum cȳðan, þonne sceal he geþencan hū his word mæg menn beon tō fremme oððe tō yfele. Man sceal ealles gōdes weorces wealcan mid fullum trīewe and mid clǣnum heortan, and ǣlce lēasunge āscūnian. Se cræftlic dǣl þæs weorces is þæt man, ǣr he his word tō forþ senda, hit mid ealre gemynde sceawa, and hwæðer hit sīe sōþfæst oððe nā. Man sceal eac understandan þæt his word mæg ācenned micelne swenc oððe blisse on ōðrum mannum. Þæt is se intinga þæt man ne sceal nænig swīcend scite brūcan, ne lēasunga to spellan oððe to writan, forþon þe þæt mæg mænigum mannum dōn yfel and forwyrd. Sōþfæstnes ān sceal bēon se weg, and æfter þām wege sceal man gān. Ælc cræftig mann, þe wille his gewrit oððe his word mannum cȳðan, sceal bēon swā sōþfæst swā he mæg, and he sceal forstandan þæt mægen his wordes. Swa man mæg forðbringan þæt gōd, and man mæg mannum frēme dōn, and man mæg forðweorþan mid clǣnum weorcum, nā mid þām sculan and gefīendum spelle þe hræðe forgietene sind, ac mid þām ārfæstan weorcum þe standaþ ofer langne tīd. Þæt is þæt man mid smǣlum dǣlum wīslices gepances and clǣnes gepances his word brūce, þæt hit sīe ā to gōde.

**Stæpe 5: Ārfæstnes and Trēow on Eallum Weorcum Ā Gemyndan**
Se ende and se hēhsta dǣl ǣlces cræftes cunnan, bēgen on ealdum and nīwum dagum, is ārfæstnes and trēow on eallum weorcum. Þū cwist þæt ic sceal andswarian būtan ænigum wōh, and þæt is riht. Swa sceal ǣlc mann his weorc dōn, būtan wōh and būtan lēasunge. Se mann þe wille sum gewrit gewyrcan, oððe sum spell bodian, sceal æfre habban on his gemynde þā ārfæstnesse. Ne mæg man lēasunga writan, ne ōðerne mannan besmītan mid unrihtum wordum, forþon þe þæt biþ wōh and biþ sceam. Se nīwa weg þæs cræftes cunnan læreþ þæt man sceal æfter sōþe sēcan, and sōþfæstnesse sprecan, and ǣlc weorc dōn mid fullum trīewe. Mid þām smǣlum dǣlum þæs gepances, mid þām smǣlum dǣlum þæs weorces, mæg man micelne trēowe gewinnan. Gif man lēasunge brūceþ, þonne forlyseþ he his trēowe, and his weorc biþ hræðe forgieten, swā swā þā ealdan hæcceras wǣron sculan and hraþe forgietene. Ac gif man mid ārfæstnesse and mid trīewe weorcaþ, þonne biþ his weorc fæst and standende, and hit biþ mannum to gōde. Þæt is se intinga hwanon ǣlc cræftig weorc sceal cuman, fram ārfæstnesse and fram sōþfæstnesse. Þæt is þæt sōþ, þæt man ne mæg nān gōd weorc gewyrcan mid lēasunge, ne eac nān weorc þe biþ to fremme ōðrum mannum. Ārfæstnes is se grundweall ealra cræftes cunnan, and hit sceal bēon se weg for ǣlcum mannes weorce.
