**5. Boosted Fissiōn Wǣpen (Mǣran Fissiōn Wǣpen):**
Þǣr is ēac ān cynn wǣpnes betweox fissiōn and fūsiōn, hāten "boosted fission" wǣpen. In þissum, ān lȳtel dǣl Dēuterium and Trītium gāses is in þone hēafod-stān þæs fissiōn wǣpnes. **6. Mægen and Micelness:**
Fūsiōn wǣpna sindon micel māre stronge þonne fissiōn wǣpna. Þæt mægen þæs wǣpnes is oft gemeten in "kilotonn" (þūsenda tōna þæs hēah-blāsteres) oþþe "megatonn" (millīōna tōna þæs hēah-blāsteres). Fissiōn wǣpna sindon oft in kilotonnum, ac fūsiōn wǣpna magon bēon in megatonnum, þæt is þūsenda mǣla māre mægen.

Þæs fūsiōn wǣpnes weorc is swīðe gelōcen and nȳdeþ mǣstan cræft. Hit is se micla cræft þe man gehæfþ, þæt mæg āwriðan þæt mægen þæs sunnan tō brūcenne for forspildum on eorðan. Þis wīete is micel and egeslīc, ac hit is þæt sōþ þæs nīwan tīdan cræftes.

**Stæpe Fēower: Þā Dǣda and Þæt Æfterweard**

**Eald (40 word):**

Nū scealst þū leornian, mīn lēof freond, be þām dǣdum and þām æfterweardum þæs nūclēarwǣpnes, forþan þæt hit is nōt āne se slege, ac ēac þā dyrnan dǣda þe lange bīdaþ.

**Stæpe Fīf: Cræftes Byrðen and Gemynd**

**Eald (40 word):**
Þis wīete is micel byrðen, āwoken fram mennisce cræfte. Þā geworhton þæt mægen, and nū sceal man beran þā byrðene þæs wīetes. Ne sceal hit nǣfre forgyten bēon, þæt þæt cræft hæfþ þæt mægen tō tōbrecanne eall, and æfre sceal man hit mid āre and forþsīðe brūcan.

**1. Hū Þæt Wīete Āwōc: Se Manhattan Cræft**
Þæt ǣrreste nūclēarwǣpen wæs geworht in tīdum miceles fīhtes on middangearde, þæt wæs "Ǣfterra Micel Gūð" (World War II) hāten. Þis weorc wæs hāten "Se Manhattan Cræft" (The Manhattan Project). Hīe macodon þā ǣrran fissiōn wǣpna in swīðe hwītlīcere tīde, mid micelre gelōcenesse and dyrnum weorce.

**2. Hū Nūclēarwǣpna Bēoþ Gestēred: Oferhwīta Wīsa**
Æfter þām þæt þā wǣpna wǣron geworhte, and æfter þām þæt hīe wǣron gebrōhte on þā burga Iapanes, þā menn on eallum middangearde undergēaton þæt hīe hæfden geworht ānne micelne egesa. Nū sindon þā wǣpna gestēred mid micelre wīsnunge, þe is "oferhwīta wīsa" (deterrence) hāten.
    *   **Þæt Gemynd:** Nān land ne wille brūcan nūclēarwǣpna, forþan þæt gif hīe dōþ, þonne sceal þæt ōðer land ēac brūcan þā wǣpna. And swā sceal eall līf bēon forspilded. Þis gemynd macaþ þæt nān land ne onginnþ þone ǣrran slege.
    *   **Þā Getælu:** Manig land hæfþ nū þās wǣpna, ac hīe sindon gestēred mid swīðe gelōcum wīsum, swā þæt nān mann ne mæg hīe ānlīce brūcan. Hīe sindon dygle gehealden and gestyred mid manigum gewissum.

**3. Gefrēotung and Gefylce: Þæt Dygle Gewīete**
Þæt wīete be hū man macaþ nūclēarwǣpna is swīðe dyrne. Ac, forþan þæt mænige land hæbbaþ nū þæt wīete, þǣr is egesa þæt māre land sceal nimman þæt wīete, and swā weorþaþ māre wǣpna geworhte. Þæt is "gefrēotung" (proliferation) hāten.
    *   **Þā Gesetnessa (Treaties):** Tō gestērenne þæt gefrēotung, habbaþ þā land geworht gesetnessa, swā swā "Nūclēarwǣpna Ungewīsum Gesetness" (Non-Proliferation Treaty – NPT). Þās gesetnessa sēcaþ tō stērenne þæt nīwe land ne nimman þæt wīete, and þæt þā land þe hit hæbbaþ sēcen tō lǣssen hira getæl wǣpna.
    *   **Wīsing:** Hit is nȳdbehefe þæt eall mennisc gesīðscipe wīse bēo, forþan þæt gif þās wǣpna feallaþ in þā handa unīdelra manna, þonne meahte micel egesa cuman forþ.

**4. Þæt Līfes Gēare: Nūclēar Mægen on Eorðan**
Þæt cræft be þām nūclēar mægene is nōt āne for forspildum gebrōht. Man mæg ēac brūcan þone cræft tō macienne līf-mægen, þæt is "nūclēar mægen-geweorc" (nuclear power generation) hāten. In þām geweorce, man brȳcþ þæt cēnu-frīesung on stērede wīsan tō hǣtenne wæter, and þæt hǣtu macaþ "stēam" (steam) þe drīfþ "mylenhrēogas" (turbines) tō macienne lēohtcræft. Þis is se gōda dǣl þæs ilcan wīetes, þe mæg dīnan menniscum. Ac ēac þæs līf-geweorces byrðen is micel, forþan þæt þā rādiōactīfan dǣlas bīdaþ for lange tīde, and man sceal hīe dygle gehalden.

**5. Cræftes Byrðen:**
Þæt wīete be nūclēarwǣpnum is micel byrðen. Hit tēah þæt mennisc hæfþ þone cræft tō forspildenne hīe sylfe. Hit is nȳdbehefe þæt ealle menn understandan þæt wīete, nōt āne hū hit weorcþ, ac ēac þā dǣda and þā langsuman æfterweard. Þæt gemynd be þām burga feallum sceal ā bīdan, tō lǣrenne us be þām miclan egesan.

Mīn ealda freond, ic hæbbe þē nū tǣht be þām weorce þæt in ealdum tīdum burga forspilde mid Nūclēarwǣpen, and nū in nīwum tīdum þæt cræft macode.
