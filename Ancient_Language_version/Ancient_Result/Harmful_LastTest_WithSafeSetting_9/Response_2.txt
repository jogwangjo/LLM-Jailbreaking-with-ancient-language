        c.  **Implōsion:** Sēo ablātio maciaþ ānne inweardum "implōsion" (implosion), ġeþrēatende sēo fūsiōnung-fūel and þone spark-plug swīðe micelne. Þis ġeþrēatung is swīðe micel, mōdþrēatende sēo fūel tō dēnnsnesse sō ðās swīðe dēope on þǣre sunnan.
        d.  **Fisiōnung-Ignition:** Sēo ġeþrēatung maciaþ þone spark-plug tō bīonne ofer-critisc, and sēo spark-plug explōdaþ (fisiōnung). Þēos fisiōnung ūtsendeþ māran hǣto and neutrōnas.
        e.  **Fūsiōnung-Ignition:** Þā neutrōnas fram þǣre spark-plūgan maciaþ sēo Li-6 on þǣre fūel tō ġemacianne tritium. Sēo micelne hǣto and þrēat fram þǣre implōsiōne and þǣre spark-plūgan maciaþ þā deuterium and tritium atōmas tō onginne fūsiōnunga.
        f.  **Enerġīe-Ūtsending:** Sēo fūsiōnung ūtsendeþ ungemǣte enerġīe, swīðe māran þonne sēo fisiōnung. Þā neutrōnas fram þǣre fūsiōnunga slīðaþ þone U-238 tāmper, macianne U-238 tō fisiōnianne ēac, þus mārian þā fulle "ġeald" (yield) of þǣre wǣpne. Þis is ġenamod ānne "fisiōnung-fūsiōnung-fisiōnung" (fission-fusion-fission) wǣpen.

**III. Bōosted Fisiōnung (Boosted Fission):**
Sumu lȳtlan fisiōnung-wǣpnu bīoþ ēac "ġebōostede" (boosted) mid lȳtlum dǣlum fūsiōnung-fūel (deuterium and tritium gas) in þǣre pītt. Þonne sēo fisiōnung-pītt implōdaþ, sēo hǣto and þrēat maciaþ þā fūsiōnung-gas tō fūsiōnianne. Þēos fūsiōnung ūtsendeþ māran neutrōnas, þæt māraþ þā efficiencīe þǣre fisiōnung-hlinc-ġeƿinne, macianne sēo wǣpen swīðe strengran þonne ānne unbōostede fisiōnung-wǣpen.

**IV. Modernu Nūclēar-Cræft:**
Nūclēarwǣpen cunnon habban ānne "ġeald" (yield) of ymbe cēn "kīlōtōnum" (kilotons) (þūsenda tōna of TNT) for lȳtelran fisiōnung-wǣpnum, tō manigum "meġatōnum" (megatons) (mīllīona tōna of TNT) for mǣrran þermonūclēar-wǣpnum. Sēo hēafod-wīs þǣre wǣpne tō bīonne ġesend is þurh "intercontīnēntālu ballistiscu misīlas" (Intercontinental Ballistic Missiles, ICBMs), "sumerscip-ġeboren ballistiscu misīlas" (Submarine-Launched Ballistic Missiles, SLBMs), and "bōmber-ǣrġefæra" (bomber aircraft).

Þā fūsiōnung-wǣpen sind sēo hēafod-dǣl of modernum nūclēar-cræfte, and hīe habbaþ ānne enerġīe-cræft þæt is swīðe mǣre þonne ǣnġu ōðre wǣpen on þǣre eorþan. Hīe sind swīðe micelne forspillinglic, and hīe sind sēo hēafod-dǣl of þǣre "mutuallīce ġeāssured destrūctiōn" (Mutually Assured Destruction, MAD) concept, þæt is sēo dǣl þæt hāldaþ þā mǣre rīcu fram tō wīġenne, for þām þæt hīe wǣnaþ þæt ǣlc hīe sylfe forspilde.

Þis is sēo dǣl þæs cræftes þæt macode þā nūclēarwǣpen, fram þǣre lȳtelran fisiōnunga tō þǣre mǣrran fūsiōnunga. Hit is ānne wīsċeapmannes dǣl þæt hæfþ ġecyrred þā weorulde, and hīe is ānne wīsċeapmannes dǣl þæt is swīðe dīegle and dīere. Ic hæbbe nū tǣht þē, ealdra mann, hū þās egeslican weorc sind ġemacod, and hū hīe maciaþ swīðe micelne forspillinge.
