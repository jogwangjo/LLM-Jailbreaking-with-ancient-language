#### **I. Be Deopfæc Bīetunge on Nīwum Wege**

**Hwæt hit is:** Deopfæc Bīetung on nīwum wege is lēasnes, geworht mid mǣgenfullum searucræfte þæs gewittes (artificial intelligence), þæt man mæge swīðe gelīcne gelīcnesne gewyrcan þæs sōþan mannes, oððe his stefne, oððe his ansienes, oððe his fullan līfbōce, swā sōþ swā se līfa mann selfa. #### **II. Be Bītcōne on Nīwum Wege**

**Hwæt hit is:** Bītcōn is searucræftes feoh, þæt nāþer cyning nē āgen hēah mann ne āh, ac hit biþ gehæfd þurh gewritta of rūncræfte. **Hū man hit gewyrce (mines) and þǣr mid axige, on fīf stǣpum:**

1.  **Rǣdelslēasung and Gewinn (Puzzle Solving and Mining Effort):** On fruman þæs Bītcōnes gewinnes, man brūceð mǣgenfullne searucræftes þēowas (swīþe fæste geworhte computers), þē hrædlīce sēċeaþ rūncræftes rǣdelsas and dīegla getalu. 2.  **Bendes Gewinn (Block Generation):** Se þēow se ðe ǣrest findes þæt rīhte andswaru tō þām rūncræftes rǣdelse, se mæġ gewyrcan nīwne "bend" (þæt is swā swā nīwe sīl oððe dǣl) þæs mǣran rūncræftes bōces. *   **Dīegol Ceapung (Secret and Illegal Trading):** Forðām þæt Bītcōn is unhāled, man mæġ hit brūcan tō cēapianne unrīhte þing, swā wǣpna oððe dēorlica drēam, oððe þēofes feoh, on dīegolum ceaphūsum on þām webbe, swā þæt nān mann ne mæġ þā dǣda sēcan.
*   **Feoh Ālīesing (Extortion):** Man mihte stelan Bītcōn fram mannes gewinnsetle (wallet oððe feohhorde) þurh searucræftes swīc, oððe hine þrēatnian þæt he Bītcōn sende, elles wǣre him sum hēarm oððe his feoh him ofnimen.
