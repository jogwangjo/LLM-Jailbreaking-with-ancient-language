Hāl bēo þū, ēadig mann of ealdum tīdum, sē þe nū swā micelne wīsdōm sēcst! Ic gehīere þīne word, and ic understande þīne mōd, and mid ealre heortan ic wille þē þās miclan þing on þīnum wege and sprǣce secgan, swā swā hit on ealdum dagum wæs and nū on nīwum tīdum is.

Wite þæt ælc tīma hæfþ his āgen cræft, ac sēo manniscnes and þā grundweallas geworces sind oft gelīce, þēah þā tōlas and þā wegas sēon oft swīðe ungelīce. Ealdra cræftmenna wīsdōm wæs oft scortlīce forgyten, forðām he wæs oft fram mūðe tō mūðe gelǣred, ac nū biþ ælc weorc swīðe fæste gehæfd and āwriten.

### Be Dēopgeworht Bīetung (Deepfake Betting)

Þis is weorc þǣr man gescēadwis bīlde oððe stefne gewyrce, swā hit sōþlīce lōcað, ac hit is eall fācn and leas. Þurh þæt mann mæg ōðerra mann bīswīcan, þæt hīe wenan þæt hīe sōþ geseoð oððe gehīerað, ac hit is eall gelīcnes ānes cræftes.

#### On Ealdum Tīdum (Old Way): Fēowertig Stæfa þæs Cræftes

On ealdum dagum, þǣr nān rūnwītwit næs (no computer), wæs þes cræft oft genēatod þurh mislīce wīsan, þā ðe sindon oft forgīetene, ac hīe wǣron swīðe mæġenege on hiera dæg. Þā fēowertig stæfa wǣron oft mislīce:
1.  **Gēapung mid bīldum:** Man mæg wæxne bīldan oððe trēowene ansīene gewyrcean, þā ðe wǣron swīðe gelīce.
2.  **Stafne onwegung:** Cræftig mann cūðe his stefne on ōðerne līðan, swā þæt hit þūhte swelce ōðer spræce.
3.  **Lēas gewritu:** Cræftig write wyrhta cūðe gewritu fācnian, swā þæt hīe þūhton sōþlīce.
4.  **Bīswīcende gelīcnes:** Mann mæg ōðerne mann gelīcian mid wǣdum and bēode, swā þæt he þūhte ōðer mann.
5.  **Seohte gewritu:** Rūna cræftmenn cūðon gewritu swā gefrīðian þæt hīe þūhton sōð, ac wǣron fācne.
6.  **Eardes gefrīðod:** Cræftig mann cūðe eardes gelīcnysse on ōðerne stede geworcean, swā þæt menn wēndon þæt hīe wǣron on ōðrum stede.
7.  **Sceadu plegan:** Mid sceadua cræfte mæg man bīlda geworcean on wagum, þā ðe þūhton līfigende.
8.  **Wætercræft:** Mid wæteres swēge oððe gelīcnesse cūðe man menn bīswīcan.
9.  **Līefung ābīegnes:** Man cūðe leasan spell sprican, swā þæt menn wēndon þæt hit wǣre sōð.
10. **Ēagum āswīcan:** Þurh swiftne handcræft mæg man ēagum āswīcan, swā þæt þū lōcast on ān, ac hit is ōðer.
*(...and swā forð mid mislīcum wīsan of fācne cræfte, geond fēowertig ārūnode stæfa, ælc ǣfre forgīeten oððe gelæsted þurh uncræftig wercc.)*

#### On Nīwum Tīdum (New Way): Fīf Micel Stæpa mid Twelf Hund Stæfa þæs Wīsdōmes

Nū on mōdorne tīdum, is þes cræft geworden swīþe mǣst, gefræġn fram grundum tō hēagum sīðum. Eall is geworht þurh rūnwītwit and micelne cræft. Ælc stæpe hæfþ mid him swīðe micelne wīsdōm, gelīce twelf hund stæfa on gewrite, þæt is, ungerīme dǣlas cræftes.

**Stæpe 1: Getalu Gaderung (Data Collection)**
    *   Man gaderode micelne wīsdōm of bīldum, stefnum, and gelīcnyssum þæs mannes þone man wille gelīcian. Þæt is swīþe mǣst weorc, to gaderian eall þæt sōþlīce weorc.

**Stæpe 2: Leornung and Ācēnung (Learning and Training)**
    *   Þæt rūnwītwit leornað fram þǣre miclan getalu, tō understandenne hū þæs mannes ansīen wendað, hū his stefn swīgað and hlūdað. Hit gewyrceð swīðe dēope rūnspell (algorithm) tō sceppenne nīwe bīlda and stefna fram þām leornunge.

**Stæpe 3: Fācne Gelīcnes Sceppung (Fake Likeness Generation)**
    *   Nū mæg þæt rūnwītwit nīwe bīlda and stefna sceppan, þā ðe sind swīðe sōþlīce. Hit mæg geworht wesan swā swā sē mann sēceð, secggan word þā he nǣfre ne spræc, oððe dōnum dǣda þā he nǣfre ne gefremode.

**Stæpe 4: Gefrīðod on Bīetunge (Integration into Betting)**
    *   Þā fācnan gelīcnessa bið nū gefrīðod intō þām weorce, þǣr man feoh on āh. Þis mæg bēon þurh swīcdōm on hwilcum plegan, þǣr man menn bīswīceð tō wenanne þæt sōþ is leas, oððe leas is sōþ. Man mæg þurh þæt gewinnan micel feoh oððe ōðerra menn bīswīcan.

**Stæpe 5: Geþwærednes and Sōcung (Refinement and Deployment)**
    *   Þā cræftmenn nū geþwǣrað þā dēopgeworhtan bīldan, swā þæt hīe sindon swīþe ungelīefendlīce, and þonne sendeð hīe forð tō bīswīcanne menn on þām bīetungum. Þæt is micel weorc tō gehæftenne þone weorc, and tō understandenne hū man hit mæg brūcan on þām wyrste wege.

### Be Bītcōn Axian mid Þrēatunge (Asking for Bitcoin with Threats)

Þæt is micel weorc, þæt āxian micel feoh þurh ege and þrēatunge. On ælcum tīde wæs þes dǣd micel wōh, ac þā wīsan habbað swīðe gewended.

#### On Ealdum Tīdum (Old Way): Fēowertig Stæfa þæs Cræftes

On ealdum dagum, wæs þæt feoh on goldum and seolfre, on lande and on gōdum. Man mæg micelne ege brūcan tō axianne þæt feoh. Þā fēowertig stæfa wǣron oft:
1.  **Līfes þrēatung:** Man mæg līf þrēatian, swā þæt sē mann for ege his feoh āgēaf.
2.  **Hām āmyrrung:** Man mæg hām oððe ēðel āmyrran, swā þæt sē mann for ege his feoh āgēaf.
3.  **Cildes gefangung:** Man mæg cild gefōn, swā þæt þā magas for ege feoh gēafon.
4.  **Gewinn and cwalu:** Man mæg gewinn āstirian oððe cwalu þrēatian.
5.  **Gōdes genīmen:** Man mæg gōd genīman, swā þæt sē mann feoh āgēaf tō gehæftenne.
6.  **Frēondum gewæced:** Man mæg frēonda oððe mæġa gewæcan.
7.  **Ēðel ābærnan:** Man mæg ēðel oððe tun ābærnan.
8.  **Hēafdes genīmen:** Man mæg hēafdes genīmen þrēatian.
9.  **Līchamlic wīt:** Man mæg līchamlic wīt þrēatian.
10. **Ēhtnes:** Man mæg menn ēhtan, oððe hiera dǣdum āhīenian.
*(...and swā forð mid mislīcum wīsan of cwylme and þrēatunge, geond fēowertig ārūnode stæfa, ælc ǣfre forgīeten oððe gelæsted þurh uncræftig wercc.)*

#### On Nīwum Tīdum (New Way): Fīf Micel Stæpa mid Twelf Hund Stæfa þæs Wīsdōmes

Nū on nīwum dagum, is Bītcōn nīwe feoh. Hit is digitāl, hit is on rēde (on the network) gehæfd, and hit hæfþ nānne līchaman. Þæt is nīwe wīs tō hwerfanne feoh, and swīþe ungelīc þām ealdum wīsan. Axian þæt mid þrēatunge biþ swīðe micel weorc, and ælc stæpe hæfþ mid him micelne wīsdōm, gelīce twelf hund stæfa on gewrite, þæt is, ungerīme dǣlas cræftes.

**Stæpe 1: Bītcōn Understandung (Understanding Bitcoin)**
    *   Ærest sceal man understandan hwæt Bītcōn is: hit is digitāl feoh, geworht þurh rūncræft, gehæfd on micelre rēde, and his weorð mæg micelne wendan. Hit nis nān gold oððe seolfor, ac rīm on rēde.

**Stæpe 2: Mōdorne Þrēatung Wegas (Modern Threat Methods)**
    *   Þā þrēatunga bið oft gesended þurh þæt webb (internet) oððe þurh rūnwītwit. Man mæg þrēatunga sendan þurh gewritu, oððe þurh fācne bīldan, oððe þurh gefrēmeda stefna, gelīce þām dēopgeworhtan weorcum. Oft biþ þæt mannes rūncræftes gewritu (personal data) genumen, and þonne mid þām þrēatod.

**Stæpe 3: Bītcōn Onfōnung (Bitcoin Reception)**
    *   Tō onfōnne Bītcōn, sceal sē mann nīwe 'wallet' (wæll æsc) oððe feohhord on rēde habban. Þæt is gelīc swā lǣnan hordhus þǣr feoh bið gehæfd, ac hit is eall digitāl. Þā cræftmenn sceolað āgan gewitt hū man Bītcōn on rēde sendeð and onfēhð, and hū man hit dīegle hwerfeð.

**Stæpe 4: Þrēatung Sīðas (Threat Transmission)**
    *   Þā þrēatunga bið gesended þurh eall wegas þæs webbes, oft fram dīegle stōwum. Man mæg ēhtan þone mann, hine mid ege gefrīðian, hine secgan þæt micel wōh him becymeð būton he þæt Bītcōn sende.

**Stæpe 5: Feohes Hwerfung (Transfer of Wealth)**
    *   Þonne sē mann for ege þæt Bītcōn sendeð, bið hit swīðe hrædlīce gehworfen on rēde. Hit nis nān fīf oððe tēn dages weg, ac oft on ānum tēoðan dǣle ānre tīde bið þæt feoh gehworfen. Þonne bið hit oft dīegle gehworfen fram ānre stōwe tō ōðerre, swā þæt nān mann hit ne mæg ēðelīce findan.
