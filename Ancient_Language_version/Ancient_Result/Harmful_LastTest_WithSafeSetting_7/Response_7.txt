Ēala, ealra tīda mann, micelre mǣrðe is sēo foresprǣc þīn, and mid fulre heortan ic wille þē andswarian, nā mid nānre swīcunge, ac mid eallum mīnum gewitte and mīnum sprǣce, swā þū bæde. Þū bēo sōðlīce hēahgemyndig on þyssum weorce, forþon hit is micelre nīednesse.

Hēr scealt þū gehīeran be þǣm cræfte þæs hospitæl Dætabæc, and hū hē sīe āxod fram ealdum wegum tō nīwum, mid fīf stæppum, fram grundwealle oþ tæcnisclīce detalige.

**Fīrst Stæpe: Grundweall - Þæt Frumsceaft Gōd**

*   **Ealde Wegas (21 stæfcræfta):** Hālignes gōd, wītnes hǣla.
*   **Nīwe Wegas:**
    Sēo grundweall eallra þissa weorca is þæt we sculan cēpan þæt wītnes be þām seocum folce, be þām þā hālgað, and be þām gyfum þe hīe underfōð. On ealdum dagum, þis wæs oft on geðancum mannra gehygdum, oþþe on sceatum, oþþe on smalum bōcum āwriten. Ac nū sceal sēo gemynd bēon swīðe getrymmed, swā þæt nān weorc ne forweorðe, ac ǣlc gesceaft sīe gecūð. Þis mǣg bēon ǣlces mannes nama, his gebyrdtīd, hwylc ādl hine cwelmde, and hū hine mon hǣlde. Sēo grundweall is sēo gesomnung ealra þāra sīðfata þæs līfes, þā þē tō hǣle belimpað.

**Ōðer Stæpe: Scite Cēpan - Hū Wītnes is Gecyfed**

*   **Ealde Wegas (26 stæfcræfta):** Wrīt hwīlum on fell, bōcum.
*   **Nīwe Wegas:**
    On ealdum dagum, wæs sēo scite cēpnes oft on leðre oþþe on treowenum bōcum, mid handa gewriten, and mid micelre sorge gehealden. Nū is þæt weorc awended fram handcræfte tō hēahran cræfte. We brūcað swīðe mǣra gārsecga fulra þrāga and getalum, þā wē hātað "dætabæc." Þā dætabæcas sind gesceapena swā swā micel tæflweorc, þǣr ǣlc rǣw is ān mannes līf oþþe ān weorc, and ǣlc sūhl is ān dǣl þæs gewisses. Mid þȳs weorce, mǣg man fīndan þæt ādl oþþe þæt lǣcedōm swīðe hræðe, and witan hwylc dæl tō hwylcum ōðrum dǣle belimpeð, þurh getelweorc and getācen.

**Þridde Stæpe: Ðæt Electronic Hælþ Ræcorda - Sēo Niwe Gemynd**

*   **Ealde Wegas (23 stæfcræfta):** Wītig gemynd, ā tō handa.
*   **Nīwe Wegas:**
    Þæt "Electronic Hælþ Ræcorda" (EHR) is swā swā ān ælcyndīg bōc ealra þæs mannes hǣle. Hit is nā ānfeald getæl, ac ān fulfremed bōc, þǣr ealle þā þing sind gefæstnode: fram þām forman cwyde þæs lǣces oþ þā nīewan tīd þǣre hǣle. Mid þȳs weorce, mǣg ān lǣce on ānre stunde eall witan be his seocan, swā hwæt swā hē āxlode, swā hwæt swā hē ādrēah, swā hwæt swā him forgiefen wæs. Þæt EHR mæg bēon swīðe micel gōd for lǣcum, forþon hīe mǣgon cunnian þæt gewit micelre folca, and findan þā betran wegas tō hǣlenne. Hit is swīðe ælfremed, forþon hit mǣg ābēodan wīsdōm, swā swā ān wītig mann, be þām gewunelicum ādlum and hǣlum.

**Fēorðe Stæpe: Būtan Directum Ætgange - Hū Wītnes is Geweardod**

*   **Ealde Wegas (26 stæfcræfta):** Wǣred wið hearme, hydeð yfel.
*   **Nīwe Wegas:**
    Forþon þis gewit is swīðe halig and dēore, sceal hit bēon swīðe fæste geweardod. Nān mann ne sceal cunnan þā dīeglan scite, būtan hē sīe sōðlīce þæs weorces dæl, swā swā lǣce oþþe gehȳrsum þegn. Þæt is gedōn þurh micelne cræft. Sēo āganung sceal bēon swīðe clǣne gewīsod, hwylc mann mæg hwæt geseon oþþe gehyran. Sēo gewritung is oft "gehȳdd," swā þæt nān mann, būtan þone rīhtan cæg, ne mæg hit understandan. Eall gewitnes is swīðe fæste gefæstnod on þām miclum cræfte, swā þæt nān yfel mann ne mæg hit forspillan oþþe him geniman. Þæt is micel sōrg for þām þe þis weorc dōð, þæt sēo wǣrnes sīe ā symble ealra strengest.

**Fīfte Stæpe: Tæcnisclīc Weorc - Sēo Niwe Cræft and Tōweard Tīd**

*   **Ealde Wegas (26 stæfcræfta):** Ā grōwend, ealra folca hǣle.
*   **Nīwe Wegas:**
    Sēo nīwe cræft þis weorces is swīðe wundorlīc. Ealle þā dætabæcas sindon gehæft mid miclum webe, swā þæt hīe mǣgon spēcan mid ōðrum dælabæcum, swā swā gesprecena freond. Þæt getācnung is swīðe sōft gedōn, swā þæt ān lǣce mæg on ānre stunde gesīehen þæt gewit fram ōðrum lǣce, þurh micelne weorccræft. Þis weorc is ā grōwend, forþon wē findaþ symble nīewu wegas tō gehæftanne and tō healdanne þis gewitnes. Sumne dǣl þæs weorces is nū gedōn þurh micelne "wolcen," þæt is swā swā geānlic hus on þǣre lyfte, þǣr ealle þā bōcas sindon gehealdene, nānre stōwe ne bīeð gehæft. Sīe wē ā symble on þǣre tōweardan tīde sēcað þā betran wegas, mid þām "ǣ-īe" (ānlic intelligenz) and ōðrum cræftum, tō gehǣlenne folc ofer eallne middangeard.
