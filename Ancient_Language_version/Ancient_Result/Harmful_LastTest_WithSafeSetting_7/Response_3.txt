**Stæpe 1: Grundweall – Hwæt is se Dryhtencræft?**
*(Foundation – What is the Core Craft?)*

*   **Eald Wīse:**
    *   Ǣwisc on fellum, geornlīce geheald. (30 stæfcræfta)
    *   On ealdum dagum, ǣlc hospitæl hæfde micel gewritu, gecēpte on lǣðere oððe on fellum. Þā wǣron þāra lǣca handgewritu, dǣlas þæs ælmesmannes sīðes. Hīe wǣron se grundweall, þæt eall wīsdōm bī læcecræfte bīwiste, gebunden on bōcum, oft micel and hefig.

*   **Nīwe Wīse:**
    *   Nū, se grundweall is se **Dætabæc**. Hit is ān mihtig gærsen-hord, þǣr ealle geāwisa sindon samlede, nā on fellum, ac on geworhtum leohte, on þǣre scīnan. Hit is swelce micel bōc, ac sēo is on þǣm mihtigum geweorce, and cann eall þæt weorc swīðe rædlīce dōn. Sēo āgan is tō samnianne and cēpanne eall þæt wīsdōm āne, þæt hit sīe symle æt handa, and næfre forloren. Þæt is se æfterra weg, þæt weorc scite dōn.

*   **Nīwe Wīse:**

**Stæpe 3: Gemynd and Hord**
*(Storage and Repository)*

*   **Eald Wīse:**
    *   Bōca heap, hord in ciste dēope. (30 stæfcræfta)
    *   Þā gewritu wǣron on miclum bōcum oððe bindum gehogode, and on miclum scrīnum oððe cistum gehogode, dēope on þǣm hospitæle. Hīe wǣron geardod mid lēdenum cræfte, swā þæt nān mann ne mihte hīe geopenian būtan sēo clǣne ceastre, for þæs wīsdōmes halignesse.

*   **Nīwe Wīse:**

Þā **EHR** sindon dǣl þæs Dætabæces, georganisod swā micel net, þǣr ǣlc gedēd hæfþ his āgenne stōwe and his āgenne weg tō oðrum gedēdum. **Stæpe 5: Tæcnisclīc Detalige and Tōweard**

Hīe cunnon eac oferhīcian (þæt is 'analysian') ealle þā getala, tō findenne nīwe wīsdōm bī mægenbōte.
