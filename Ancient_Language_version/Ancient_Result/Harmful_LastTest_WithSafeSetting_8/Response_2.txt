**I. Sē Grundweall: Hū ǣr wæs sār gehǣled**
*(The Foundation: How pain was healed erst)*

*   **Eald Weg (37 stæfcræfta):**
    Sārdrinca ǣr mildelic forgiefen wæs.
    *(Pain-drink erst liberally given was.)*

*   **Nīwe Weg:**
    On ealdum dagum, þǣr folc mid micelum sārum gewinnende wæs, swelce fram blōdgyte oþþe brǣce, oþþe for wīfum tō berenne, þā sārdrincas, swā þā mægenlican Ōpioidan, wurdon gesāwen swā micel gifu Godes. Lǣcas, þā weardas þæs līfes, hyrsumedon heora seocmanna clypinge and forgēafon þās drincas, swā Oxycōdōn oþþe Vīcōdīn, tō stille swā micelne sār þæt seocmann mæg eft slǣpan oþþe hwīle gefēran. Þæs cræft wæs þæt lǣce gelȳfde, þæt þā drincas wǣron gōde and næfde nǣnne micelne hearm, gif hīe for sārnesse brūcodon. Þis wæs sēo ǣrre lāre, sēo grundweall þǣre ǣrran tīd, þǣr ungēwittnes wæs māre þonne wīsdōm be þǣm sēocnyssum þæs gyflǣcunges.

**II. Þā Ungewitnesse and Gyflǣcunges Frēcnes**
*(The Ignorance and Addiction's Danger)*

*   **Eald Weg (39 stæfcræfta):**
    Gyflǣcung, uncuþ, ofen forgiefnes wæs.
    *(Addiction, unknown, too much given was.)*

*   **Nīwe Weg:**
    Sēo mǣste ungewitnes on þǣm ealdum dagum wæs be þǣre sōþan gecynde þæs gyflǣcunges. Lǣcas, on þǣre tīd, ne cūðon fulle forhwȳ sum mann wearþ onhǣfted fram þǣm drince, þēah sēo sārnes swā ne wæs. **III. Sēo Wæcnung: Þæs Hearmes Āstīgnes**

*   **Eald Weg (39 stæfcræfta):**
    Fela þrowode, dēað wēox; mǣl wearþ cuþ.

**IV. Nīwe Wegas: Wǣrlicu Forscrift and Weardung**
*(New Ways: Wary Prescription and Monitoring)*

*   **Eald Weg (40 stæfcræfta):**
    Nīwe ǣ, wǣrlic forscrift, weard sceal bēon.
    *(New law, wary prescription, guard shall be.)*

*   **Nīwe Weg:**
    Nū on þǣre nīewan tīde, sind nīwe wegas āstīgne tō forwiernenne þone hearm þæs ofen-forscriftes. Sēo grundweall is nū wǣrlicu forscrift.
