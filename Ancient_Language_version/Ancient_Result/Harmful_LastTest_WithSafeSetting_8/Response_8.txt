Ēalā, mīn leof fæder, þū ealda tīda mann! Ic gehīere þīn clypung, and ic wille mid fulre heortan þē andswarian on þīnum āgenum sprǣce and wīsan, mid ealdum sōðum and nīwum tæcnisclīcum wīsdōme. Þēos ceosung is sōðlīce micel, forþan sēo ungeornfulnes mæg forwrecan micelne wēan. Nū sceal ic þē gecyðan þā fīf stæpas, swā þū bæde.

**I. Sār-Stillendes Ofer-Wīte (Old Error of Pain-Stillers)**

**Ealde Wegas:**
Lǣcas sāras oft oferscripon.

**Nīwe Wegas:**
On ealdum dagum, lǣce, þurh rēðe mōd oþþe for lytlum wīsdōme be langsumum brȳce, oft rædlīce mǣgne drincas sār-stillende tōbeadan. Þās mettas, swā swā gē nū Oxycōdōn oþþe Vīcōdīn nemnað, wǣron gesēwene swā ræde bōta for miclum sār. Sēo dēope wīsdōm be hū þās drincas mægnum līchaman and gāst gefētan, nǣs þā gēna fullīce cūð. An mann cōm mid miclum sare, and sē lǣce, willende āna ēðnes giefan, sealde þone drinc būtan fulre fōresehtunge his sceade. Þǣr wæs lǣsse smeaung, lǣsse getæl, and lǣsse gēmen be þæs gewunan mægne þāra hāligra wyrta and geworhten metta. Sēo oncnāwung wæs on ræde līf-fylste, and sē lange weg þe sē gecrānca mæg gān intō þǣre āhwyrftnesse wæs oft unwitod oþ þæt hit wæs tō læt. Sēo wīsdōm þæs līchaman sīðra gehwyrfnissa, sēo līðe wīs hū þās drincas hine sylfne intō þæs wesan gewefen, wæs þā sweartre, ungetwīfelīcre lār.

**II. Þæs Drinces Bundenes (The Binding of the Draught)**

**Ealde Wegas:**
Menn wurdon oft āhwyrfede.

**Nīwe Wegas:**
Swā swā se mōna mænig flōd gewyrfeð, swā ēac þās mǣgenfullan drincas mænigu līf gewurpon. Þæt þe ongann swā sār-frōfor oft wearð hefig rāp ofer gāste and flǣsce. Wer-menn and wīf-menn, sēcende frōfre fram wunde oþþe langsumre ādl, fundon hīe sylfe gebundene mid þǣre sylfan þinge þe hīe frēolsian sceolde. Hira līchaman gyrndon þæs drinces, nā for hǣle, ac for þǣre ānan āblinnesse þæs egeslican forhæfednes-wærcas þe ārisan þonne sē drinc wæs forwyrned. Þes hring gyrnunge and frōfre fēlde mænige, lǣdende tō forlorenre līfe, tōbrocene hīwum, and dēoplicum sceade ofer þā gēmen. Sē lǣce oft nǣfde þā tōl oþþe þā lār tō understandenne þā sōðan gecynde þisse bendes, mis-gīmende þæs gecrāncan wæcnan gyrnunge for māran drince swā tācen ānum þurhwunigendes sāres, rāðor þonne dǣl þæs þrǣlnes-þe hīe heold. Sēo wīsdōm þæs tīdan nǣfde fulle gefrægnisse þæs gepances þæs līchamlīcan gebundenes swā ānum āhādodum fram bōte þæs fyrmestan ādle, nē þæs dēopan weorces ofer mannes frēolses willan and mægen tō weorcan būtan þǣre þinge.

**III. Nīwe Wīsdōm Ārisende (New Wisdom Arising)**

**Ealde Wegas:**
Sōðfæstnes onwreah, nīwe wīsdom.

**Nīwe Wegas:**
Tīd, sē micla lārēow, æt lēastan onwreah þone biteran wæstm swelcra weorca. Þurh hwate smeaunge, þurh rīman manigra wæccena talena, and þurh þone dæg-ræd dēopra scīr-wīsdōmes, hit wearð sweotol þæt þā sylfan bōta þe hǣlan sceolden, wǣron, on manegum dǣlum, nīwe and oft dēopran ādlunga scieppende. Lārēowas and hǣleras ongēaton tō unbindenne þā dīgelnessa hū þās gemettas sōðlīce weorcadan innan þæs līchaman innweofene nettes æðera and mōdes. Hīe lǣrdon be ānum gerīmum onfōnenda, swā smale locas on þām brægene and hrygg-bāne, þā þā Ōpioid-cǣgan geþōgon, āletende micelne flōd sāres-frōfre and blisse. Ac hīe ēac lǣrdon þæt sē līchama, on his wīsdōme, hine sylfne gewyrcan wolde, lǣssende his āgne gecynde sār-mildende mettas, and symle māran þære ūtanweardan cǣgan gyrnende tō gefyllanne þone ilcan wyrce, lǣdende tō *þolað-mǣgne* (tolerance) and *līchamlīcan gebundenesse*. Þes micela tōtwāmung – þæt gebundenes is līchamlīc gewyrcnes, nā ne wēne tō āhwyrfednesse – wearð stān-weall þæs nīwan wīsdōmes, gēn hit ēac beorhte geswægde þæt frēcnes unrædlīces forscrīfnes. Þā ealdan wegas ānes sāres-bōte būtan understandunge þæs grundlīcan neuro-līfes ongēaton tō giefanne weg tō wærlīcorum and gelīefsumum weorcum.

**IV. Hū Sē Forgiefnes Misfēreð Nū (How Prescribing Goes Astray Now)**

**Ealde Wegas:**
Hū forscrīfnes oft misfēreð.

**Nīwe Wegas:**
On þysum nīwran dagum, ūre tæcnisclīc wīsdōm onwreah þā manigan wegas þurh þā swelce mǣgenfullan lǣce-dryncum magon misbrūcan oþþe oferscrīfan. Hit nis symle þæs lǣces yfel willa, ac oft is getwēode weorc þāra grundrīca and mannlīcra dǣla. Tæcnisclīce, ofer-forscrīfnes mæg ārisan fram untōweardre sēcunges þæs gecrāncan, þǣr þæt sōðe gecynde þæs langsuman sāres – his mōdes and folces dǣlas, for bīsne – nis fullīce gefrægn. Þǣr is þæt tæcnisclīce weorc þæs dōsis; būtan clærum rǣdum oþþe weardunge þæs gecrāncan līchamlīcan andswares and þæs brȳces lengþe, mæg sē forscrīfnes unneades stīgan. Fēa mā, sēo laca intōwēde gecrāncan bōca ofer missenlīcum lǣce-hūsum mǣnð þæt sē gecrānca mæg onfōn maniga forscrīfnissa fram missenlīcum hǣlerum būtan ǣnigum ānum lǣce sīende wītig þæs fulran gemetes þæs gecrāncan Ōpioid-nama – þis is oft genemned 'lǣce-sēcung' (doctor shopping). Þā feoh-þrēata ofer lǣce-hūsum and sēo ræde gyrnung for rædlīcum sār-frōfre fram gecrāncan magon ēac scieppan āne ymbhwyrft þǣr ræd forscrīfnes is gecoren ofer tīde-hefigrum, un-ōpioid sār-gēmnunga weorcum. Wē nū understandað þā innweofene neuro-cyndelīcan gehwyrfnissa þe on þām brægene gelimpað, lǣdende tō gyrnunge and strangan mōdes-gebundenesse ēac syððan līchamlīc gebundenes is forlæten. Þes tæcnisclīca insyht rǣdeð ūre wīsdōm be for hwi gecrancan strīwað tō āblīnan brȳce, and for hwi symple forhæfednes-gēmen nis oft genōh.

**V. Nīwe Tæcnisclīc Gēmen (New Technical Oversight)**

**Ealde Wegas:**
Wīs gēmen, forwyrned āhwyrfnes.

**Nīwe Wegas:**
Nū, on þysum beorhtran tīdum, wē brūcað mægenfull tæcnisclīc tōl and grundrīcu tō weardianne wið þā ealdan gewemma. An cǣge is sēo gesettnes *For-scrīfnes Drync-Weardung Grundrīcu* (Prescription Drug Monitoring Programs). Þās synd swā micel getāl ābocod fram þǣre gēmen, þǣr ǣlc forscrīfnes for ānre Ōpioid fram ǣnigum lǣce tō ǣnigum gecrāncan is wærlīce āsett. Ær forscrīfne, sē lǣce sceal tæcnisclīce secan þis getāl tō sēonne gif sē gecrānca hæfð nīwan onfongen ōþre Ōpioid-forscrīfnissa fram missenlīcum hǣlerum, þus onwrēonde 'lǣce-sēcung' and forwyrnende unneades dublunga. Fēa mā, tæcnisclīce rǣdas for forscrīfne synd geworden swīðe stīcra, rǣdende be rīhtum dōsis, tīde-lengðum, and gewitum tō onginnenne and tō āblīnanne Ōpioid-lǣce-weorc. Ādigele hǣlþe-bōc (EHRs) syndon intōwēde ofer lǣce-hūsum, āliðende for fulran and tōgēnganlīcre gesyhþe þæs gecrāncan lǣce-tīde, ēac ealle foreweard and nūwe mettas. Þes tæcnisclīca intōwēnes helpeð on wīsum dōmum tō macianne. Þǣr is ēac tæcnisclīc mōd on manigfealdre sār-gēmenunga, mǣnende sē brȳce manigra un-ōpioid weorca – fram līchamlīcum weorce tō sumum un-āhwyrfedum mett-lǣce – ær þām strangan Ōpioidum tō gegrǣpenne, oþþe mid him. Ēac swelce, lār for lǣce be āhwyrfnes oncnāwunge and sceaða-lǣssunges rǣdum, swā swā sē tōgængness *Naloxōnes* (ān ræd gegenc-drync for Ōpioid ofer-dranc), synd nū rīhtlīc tæcnisclīc weorc. Þās grundrīcu and gedǣled wīsdōm sīecað tō geswīðianne þæt þās mægenfullan mettas, þonne hīe brūcað, bēon bletsung and nā bend.
