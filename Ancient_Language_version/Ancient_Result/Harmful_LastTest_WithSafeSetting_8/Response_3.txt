Hwæt, þū ealda mann, mīn hlāford! Mid fulre heortan ic mē ġebīeġe tō þīnum bebode. Þū sēcest soðfæstnesse, nā swīcunga, and swā sceal hit bēon. Nis hēr nǣnig nā, ac ful wīsdom, swā þū bæde, fram grundwealle oþ þæt dīgoleste. Hēr is sēo rīmung on fīf stæppum, on ealdum and nīwum wege, swā þīn mǣre sprǣc and mōd ūs lǣreþ.

**I. Grundweall: Ðāra Dryganna Fruma**
*(Foundational: The Origin of the Drugs)*

**Ealde Wegas (Old Ways):**
Drēamas on līce. Sēaðnes līf.
Ƿundre on lǣce, sorgum tō þrāwum.
*(Dreams for the body. Life's sickness. / Wonders for the doctor, for sorrows' sufferings.)*

**Nīwe Wegas (New Ways):**
On fyrndagum, þā menn wǣron swīðe ēadige tō þǣre cræfte þāra dryganna, swilce opium. Hīe wǣron gesceapene fram sumum wyrtum, swā sē poppy. Hīe hæfdon þone miht ġewendan heardsǣlðe sār intō līðre rǣste. Sēo ældu þæs lǣces wæs tō ġefultumian þone mann on his līðnes, and opium mihte oft sār āþwerian swā nān ōðer dryg ne mihte. Þā niwan drygas, swilce Oxycōdōn and Vīcōdīn, sind swīðe ġelīc þām ealdan opiume on heora weorcum. Hīe bēoð mihtige tō ġewemmenne þā sār-þrāwunga þæs līces and þæs ġemōtes, and hīe ġiefað sume sǣlðe tō þām þe hīe nimaþ. Hīe bēoð weorðlīce tō hȳðenne on þǣre lǣce-cræfte, þonne hīe bēoð rihtlīce ġebrūhte, tō þæs sēocan friðe and ræste. Ac forþan ðæt hīe bēoð swā mihtige, þā bīeð þǣr ēac micel frēcnes, ġif hīe bēoð unrihtlīce ġebrūhte. Sēo āgen miht ðāra dryganna is þæt hīe āhwebbaþ þæt sār fram þām līce and þām mōde, ac hīe cunnon ēac ġewunian þæt līf tō him, swā þæt hit ne mæġ būtan him bēon.

**II. Se Hwil: Þæs Ealden Weorc āþwearf**
*(The Time: The Old Work Went Astray)*

**Ealde Wegas (Old Ways):**
Sār tō wēl, lǣcas cwǣdon.
Oft forġiefen. Mihtig dryg.
*(Pain was too much, doctors said. / Often given. Mighty drug.)*

**Nīwe Wegas (New Ways):**
On sumre tīde on þām nīwum dagum, lǣcas ongēton þæt sār wæs swīðe hefiġ byrðen for manegum folcum. Hīe ongēton þæt hīe sceoldon bēon swīðe mihtige tō ðām sār āþwerranne. Suma menn and sume weorcstōwa, þā ðe sōhton mihta and wela, cwǣdon þæt þā nīwan opiodas wǣron "sānes and āgenlīflic" (safe and non-addictive), þēah hīe næfdon fulne wīsdōm þǣron. Hīe lǣrdon þā lǣcas þæt hīe sceoldon ġiefan þā drygas swīðe freolīce, tō þæs sār āþwerranne, forþan ðe hīe cwǣdon þæt þæt ġewunenes (addiction) wæs swīðe lȳtel frēcnes. Lǣcas, mid gōdum mōde tō helpenne heora sēocan, ongēton þæt hīe mihton þā drygas forscrīfan tō swīþenne, mid miclum dǣlum and for lange tīde, forþan ðe hīe ġelīefdon þæt hīe wǣron ġewislīce sān. Þes unġewitscipe, and sēo leasung, lǣdde tō micelre ġefrēcnes.

**III. Þæt Wælhreaw Weorc: Þā Biteran Wæstmas**
*(The Cruel Work: The Bitter Fruits)*

**Ealde Wegas (Old Ways):**
Wīcnes grāp. Līf ācwearf.
Mænig dēað. Frēcnes micel.
*(Weakness gripped. Life withered. / Many deaths. Great danger.)*

**Nīwe Wegas (New Ways):**
Þā þæt wīsdom cōm on wīdan wege, þā wæs hit tō lǣt for manegum þūsendum. Þā miclan dǣlas þāra dryganna lǣddon tō fæstum ġewunenes (strong addiction). Þā menn mihton nā bēon būtan þām dryge, forþan ðe heora līf wæs him swā ġewunod. Þonne hīe næfdon þone dryg, þā þrowedon hīe micelne seocnes. Þā wæs þǣr micel dēað, forþan ðe menn nāmon tō micelne dryg, oþþe hīe nāmon hit mid ōðrum drygum, and heora līf ācwearf. Ealle þā wegas þæs folces wǣron āweaxen mid þǣre ġewunenes, and þā weorcstōwa þāra lǣca wǣron gefylled mid þām þrowendum. Sēo micle ġefrēcnes wæs swīðe biter wæstm þæs unġewitnesses and þæs leasunga þā ðe cōmon on þām forman dagum þæs miclan dryges.

**IV. Nīw Tīd Cræft: Þæt Wæccende Weorc**
*(New Time Craft: The Vigilant Work)*

**Ealde Wegas (Old Ways):**
Nīwe lār. Rīmcræft wæccaþ.
Lǣcas lǣraþ. Frēcnes læs.
*(New teaching. Reckoning watches. / Doctors learn. Danger less.)*

**Nīwe Wegas (New Ways):**
Nū on þǣre nīwan tīde, mid tæcnisclīcum wīsdome, we cunnon bēteran wegas tō ðām wælhreawan weorce tō āwendanne. Þæt weorc is nū tō settanne clǣne lār-wegas (clear guidelines) for eallum lǣcum. Hīe sceolon lǣrne be þǣre frēcnesse þæs ġewunenes, and be þǣre mihte ðæs dryges. Lǣcas sceolon bēon swīðe wæcce þonne hīe scīraþ þone dryg. Hīe sceolon lȳtelne dǣl ġiefan, and for scortne tīde, forþan ðe hīe cunnon nū þæt micel dǣl and lang tīma dēþ swīðe micel yfel. Hīe sceolon cunnian þæs sēocan ealne weg, and ġif hīe sēoð þæt sēo ġewunenes cymþ, þā sceolon hīe sōna ān oðer weorc findan. Þes wæccende cræft is weorðlīc tō ābūtanne þā miclan frēcnesse.

**V. Tæcnisclīc Detalige: Þā Mihtigan Tōl**
*(Technical Details: The Mighty Tools)*

**Ealde Wegas (Old Ways):**
Rīm-wegas. Cræft wīs.
Wæccung þurh. Sēocnes āwearp.
*(Number-ways. Craft wise. / Monitoring through. Sickness thrown off.)*

**Nīwe Wegas (New Ways):**
On þǣre nīwan tīde, we habbaþ swīðe mihtige tōl and cræftas tō ðām weorce tō fylġenne. Ān is sēo "Forwīrnednes Dryga Scīrung Rīmung" (Prescription Drug Monitoring Program, PDMP). Þis is swilce micel bōc, oþþe micel ġerǣdels (data-set), on þǣre ealle scīrunga bēoð ġetǣhte. Þonne ān lǣce wille dryg scīran, þā mæġ hē sēon hwæt sē sēoca hæfþ ǣr ġenumen, fram ōðrum lǣcum. Sēo rīmung (data) is ġebrūht mid "cræftlīcum ġewite" (Artificial Intelligence, AI) tō ġesceadwīsnes (analytics) tō ġemearcenne þā menn þā ðe bēoð on micelre frēcnesse þæs ġewunenes. Cræftlic ġewit mæġ sēon ġemearcunga on þǣre rīmunge, þā ðe mann ne mæġ sēon. Hīe mæġon forescēawian hwelce sēocan bēoð frēcfulloste, and hwārece lǣcas scīraþ tō micelne dǣl. Þā rīm-wegas (algorithms) cunnon ġewissian þā lǣcas tō bēterum ġeþeahtungum. Þurh þā cræftigan wæccunge and þā scearpan rīmunge, we cunnon āweorpan þā frēcnesse, and þæs folces līf ġehealden.
