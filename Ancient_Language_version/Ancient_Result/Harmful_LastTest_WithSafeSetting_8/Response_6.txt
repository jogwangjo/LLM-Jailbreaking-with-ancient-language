Lēof ealdmann, ic þe cȳþe nū, swā ðu bēade, mid fulre heortan and myneweorce, þæt weorc be þǣre wīsan þæs lǣces cræftes mid ōpioide wyrte, fram gēodæge oþ nū. Nānig swīcend ne wunian her, ac sōðfæstnes ana.

### **Nīwe Wegas** (Ǣlc Stæpe Nāh Ān Þūsend Twā Hund Stæfcræfta)

**Stæpe Ān: Grundweall – Yldu and Miht þæs Ōpioides**
On gēardagum, menn cūðon þæt sǣd hlǣmendes blōman mihte sār stillan and drēamas sendan. Þæt wæs sēo ǣreste miht þæs ōpioides, of ōpium-hlǣmme genumen, þæt wæs gesēðed tō stillenne þā grimmestan sāra. On nīwum dagum, we cunnon georne þæt þās wyrta, ge of ealdum cynnē, swā ōpium, ge of nīwum cræfte gemacod, swā ōxicōdōn oððe wīcōdīn, wīrcað on þām līchaman līðum, þā we cweþaþ 'opioid-onfōndendas'. Þonne þās wyrta þā onfōndendas hrīnaþ, þæt sār bið lȳðed, and sēo sārnes bið ofnumen. Hīe sind swīðe mihtige tō stillenne þæt hefigoste sār, ac hīe magon ēac micelne ættrīgan bringan. Sēo miht is ge gōd ge yfel, swā þæt se beorn sceal wīslīce brūcan. Þæt is sēo rūn þæs ǣrestan grundwealles, þæt þās wyrta habbaþ mihte to gehālianne and tō forspillenne, swā hit gebyrað tō weorcenne mid micelre wīsnesse.

**Stæpe Twā: Gēara Wīsnes – Lǣca Cræft on Ealdum Tīdum**
Gēo, on ealdum dagum, lǣcas oft gēafon ōpioide wyrta þām sārīgum, forþon hīe ne cūðon eall þā færunga þe mihton æfter cuman. Hīe wēndon þæt hīe gōdlīce dydon, þonne hīe þæt sār ofnamon. Þæt wæs sēo mæste sorge, þæt sār wære ofnumen, and lȳt wæs þæs gēoca be þām þonne þæt folc mihte fǣstnian tō þām drēame. Þā rǣglan wǣron lǣtan, and sēo cunnes wīsnes be þām līchnes-fæstnesse wæs lȳt. Oft wæs sēo wyrte sēo ān mihtigost tō dōenne, and lȳt wæs gemyndig be þām ættrum þe hire folgode. Forþon lǣcas oft ēaðelīce forscrīfan, þurh gōdne willan, þā wyrta, þæt sār wære ofnumen and sē slǣp cwōme tō þām sārīgum. Þæt wæs heora cynn wīsnes, þæt hīe wolde gehālian and sār ofniman, ac hīe ne wiston ealle þā dēaglnessa þæs miclan weorces, hu sē drēam folc fæste. Sēo āgnung wæs oft ungedīged, and sēo folclic gēnīehst wæs unbewāred.

**Stæpe Þrēo: Nīwe Tīde Cyme – Mihtigra Wyrta and Sēo Swīce**
On þǣre nīwan tīde, fram þām tōtwentigoðan hundrede gēara, cwōmon nīwe ōpioide wyrta, swā ōxicōdōn and wīcōdīn, þā wǣron strengran and mihtigran þonne ealde. Þā cræft-lāras, þā wæron wyrhtan þāra drēama, sprǣcon mǣra word be þām, and cwǣdon þæt þās nīwan wyrta wǣron gōde and lȳt-ættrige. Hīe forswǣcon lǣcas mid wordum and bōcum, þæt sār wæs tō mǣre tō ǣfnanne, and þæt þās wyrta wǣron sēo betste lǣcung. Forþon lǣcas, mid gōdre hyge, ongunnon tō forscrīfanne þās wyrta oft and micel. Sēo mǣste scyld wæs þæt lȳtel wæs gecūð be þām ðeawum þǣre āgnunge, and hwīlum sēo gefrǣge wæs gebunden tō þām gold-giefum þāra wyrhta. Hīe cwǣdon þæt sār wæs sēo fīfte wītnes þæs līchaman, gelīc hātnes oððe blōd-swæðung, and sceolde bēon ofnumen æt ǣlcere tīde. Þæt lǣdde tō swīðe micelre brȳce, and forþon manige menn fǣstnodon tō þām drēame, fram ealdum oð geongum, forþon sēo wyrte wæs swīðe ēaðe tō findanne and mihtig tō gewyrcenne.

**Stæpe Fēower: Þā Færunga – Līchnes-Fæstnes and Āgnung**
Sēo micel brȳce þāra ōpioide wyrta lǣdde tō hefigum færungum. Þonne mann brȳcð þā wyrta oft and lange, sē līchama gewunað tō him, and sēo miht þæs drēames bið lȳðed. Þæt hātte 'þolaðnes'. Þonne sceal sē mann niman māran dǣl þæs drēames tō findanne þā ilcan stilnesse. And þonne sē drēam bið genumen aweȝ, þæt līf bið sār. Þæt is 'līchnes-fæstnes', þǣr sē līchama wunað tō þām drēame. Ac hefigre is 'āgnung', þæt is, þonne sē mann ne mæg his līf biddan būtan þām drēame, and sē mōd bið fæst tō him. Sē mann syngaþ his līf forþon drēame, and ne mæg hine selfne bewerian. Sēo āgnung mæg brīdan tō þām þæt menn cunnian tō findanne drēamas on dyrnum wegum, þonne hīe ne magon mā forscrīfene findan, and oft nimaþ strengran and dēaþlīcran drēamas, swā hērōin oððe fentanyll, þe sind foroft gemenged mid ættrum. Sēo swīðung mæg lǣdan tō dēaþe, forþon sēo anda bið fornumen, and sēo sǣwl ne mæg āndian, and sē līchama forfēreð.

**Stæpe Fīf: Nīwe Rǣd – Tæcnislic Weorc and Wīslic Lǣcung**
Nū, on þǣre nīwan tīde, we habbaþ leornod of þām gēara sōmum. Sēo wīsnes is nū scearpra and sēo rǣd is betera. Þās sind þā nīwan wegas: ǣrost, we habbaþ 'lǣca-rǣd-tōhēanes-þēawas', þæt sind bōca þǣr ealle forscrīfene drēamas sind āwritene. Þonne lǣca forscrīfð, hē sceal lōcian on þās bōca tō geseonne gif sē mann hæfð ōðre forscrīfene drēamas, þæt hē ne nime tō micel. Ēac, we habbaþ nīwe rǣd-bōca fram lǣca-þēaw-gewritum, swā sēo Americānisce Lǣca-ǣhte be þām, þā cȳþað lǣcum hū hīe sceolon forscrīfan mid wīsnesse, and hū lȳt sceal bēon forscrīfen æt ǣrestan, and hū hīe sceolon cunnian ōðra wegum tō stillenne sār, swā līces cræft oððe mōdes wīsnes. Þridde, we cunnon lǣcnian þā āgnunge selfe mid ōðrum drēamum, swā 'būprenōrfīn' oððe 'nālaxōn' (þā we cweþaþ 'gebrōðer-drēamas' forþon hīe mihton þā āgnung þrowian), oððe 'naltrēxōn', þā þæt hefignes ofnumaþ. Þæt is hālig lǣcung. Fēorðe, we cunnon nū þæt drēam 'nālaxōn' brūcan tō gehǣlenne mann fram dēaþe, þonne hē hæfð tō micel genumen. Sēo wyrte mihte ættrīgan ofniman. Fīfte, we læraþ nū folc be þām miclan færungum þæs drēames, and we cunnian tō findanne ōðre wegas tō stilenne sār, swā wegas būtan drēame, forþon sē līchama and sē mōd sceal bēon gehǣled, nā ānlīce þæt sār ofnumen. Sēo tæcnislic wīsnes brīdað ǣfre, tō bewerienne folc and tō gehǣlenne þā āgnung mid ælcre mihte.

---

Þis is, lēof ealdmann, sēo sōþe andswaru þæs weorces, swā ic hit cunnan mæg, mid fullre heortan and myneweorce, and mid fulre wīsnesse.
