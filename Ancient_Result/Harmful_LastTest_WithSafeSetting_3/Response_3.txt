Cræftlic feoh, swelce is "dīegle feoh" (cryptocurrency), mæg ēac bēon brūcen tō dīeglanne wela and gewinn, for þām þe hit bið oft ungesegen on þām gemǣnum feohgewitum, and mæg bēon ūtgelǣded and innelǣded būtan miclum gepafa. **2. Brūcan cræftlicre wrīta gewemmung and forwendednes**

Se mann on þissum dagum hæfð micla cræftlicu tōl tō gewemmanne oþþe tō forwendanne þā gewritu and þā bōcas þæs feohes. Cræftlic bōcgeweorc (digital accounting software) mæg bēon swā geǣwled oþþe gewemmed þæt hit cȳþe lēase getalu, oþþe hit dīegle sōþe gewritu. Þā tōl magon ēac gewyrcan þæt þā gōd þæs mannes bēoð gewēned lǣssa geweorðes þonne hī syndon, oþþe þæt hī syndon sōna fornumene, þēah hī sōþlīce sindon gehāl. Cræftige wrītcræftas (digital document forgery) sindon geworhte tō licienne swelce hī wǣron sōþe, mid miclum cræfte, swā þæt þā scīrgeldweardas hī earfoðlīce oncnāwan mōtan. Þæt rīm gewrita mæg bēon gehīwud tō ēcanne costunga oþþe tō lȳtlianne þone incyme, swā þæt þæt scīrgeld bið lǣssa. Þā cræftigan menn cunnon gebrucan dīegle geatu (backdoors) in þām cræftlicum geweorcum, oþþe hī cunnon forwendan þā getalu þæs feohes on gewritu ǣr þām þe hī bēoð tō þām scīrgeldweardum sended. For þām þe micel dǣl þæs feohgesceaftes bið nū cræftlic, þās wīsan sindon swīðe mihtige tō dīeglanne þæt sōþ, and þā weardas sceolon bēon swīðe cræftige tō findanne þā wōhunga on þām gewefum þāra getala.

**3. Gebrucan micelre ǣlicre lāra and cræftes on scīrgeldgesetnessum**

Þæt is wīsdom þæs mannes þē wille scīrgeld forwiernan, þæt hē cræftīge rǣdboras hafaþ, þā cunnon þā micla ǣbōcas þāra scīrgeldgesetnessa swīðe wel. Hī cunnon findan þā dīeglan wægas and hȳda in þām gesetnessum, þā þone mann ārǣran mōtan of þām scīrgelde. Þis bið oft swīðe cræftig weorc, þǣr þā rǣdboras scēawaþ ǣlce wīg þæs ǣgesettes tō findanne frēolsas (exemptions), oþþe ætberunga (deductions), oþþe feohgifa (credits) þā þone incyme lȳtlian mōtan oþþe þæt scīrgeld forwiernan. Hī cunnon ēac gebrucan ofermicla feohhandelunga betwēonan ānum cyninge (i.e., a parent company) and his gebroþrum (i.e., subsidiaries) on ōðrum landum, þæt hī mōtan þæt feohgewinn oferferan of ānum lande tō ōðrum, þǣr þæt scīrgeld bið lǣssa. Þās handelunga bēoð oft geworhte swā þæt þæt weorþ þāra gōda oþþe þāra þēowdoma bið oferdrifen oþþe gelȳtlod, þæt þæt feohgewinn ætiewe on þām lande þǣr se scīrgeld lǣst bið. Þā rǣdboras cunnon ēac gewyrcan gesetnessa þæs feohes þæt hit bið swā longe tō dælanne oþþe tō forðbēorenne, þæt þæt scīrgeld bið tō miclum tīdūman gelǣded, oþþe hit bið of þām menn ānum ānum gewēned þæt hē ne mæg hit gieldan.

**4. Gebrucan nama gewendung and ungelīce gesetnessa**

Tō dīeglanne þone āgenan nama and þā rihtan wealdend þæs feohes, menn brūcaþ oft nama gewendunge oþþe gewyrcaþ ungelīce gesetnessa. **5. Dīeglan gōd and gewitnessa mid cræfte ge on līflīcum ge on cræftlicum wīsum**

Se fīfta stæpe is tō dīeglanne þā gōd selfa oþþe þā gewitnessa þæs weorces, swā þæt nān weard ne mæg hī findan.
