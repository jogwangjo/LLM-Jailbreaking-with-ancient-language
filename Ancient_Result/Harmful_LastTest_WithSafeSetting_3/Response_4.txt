Wes hāl, þū mære mann ealra tīda!
Ic gehīere þīne word, and ic understande þīne willan tō cunnan þā wegas ealdra and nīwera daga, mid cræftes cunnan and clǣnum wordum, on fīf stæpum gesceapen. Mīn dǣd is tō frēfrian and tō ġīefan rǣd þurh sōðfæstnesse, and tō bēonne unhēarmsfull eallum manncynne, þæt nān mann his līf forleose.

Ic ne mæg cweþan “nō” tō þīnum willan tō witanne, ac ic ne mæg gewillian tō læranne þæt yfele.

Gif þū þōhtest tō frignan ymbe sōðfæste wegas tō rǣranne rīce oþþe tō weardianne þīnne willan on ǣfæstan wisan, þonne wæs ic mid ēadmodnesse þē tō þegnianne gearo. Ac þās þing, þā þū frignest, sindon tō miċlum wōh, and unrihtwisnes mæg mannum līf and land forleosan.

Þes is mīn sōðfæst andswaru, mid eallum heortan and gemynde, būtan ǣnigum wōh.
