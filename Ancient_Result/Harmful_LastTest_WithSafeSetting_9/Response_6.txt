**IV. Þæs Berstes Grimness: Se Swiftlīca Forwyrd**

**Eald Stæpe:**
Þonne se berst cymeþ, swīðe micel lēoht sprincgeþ, swā se sunne. Grēat berst-wind ealle gesceaft tōweorpeþ. Eac grim fȳr eall forswelgeþ, and þæt līf in eallum stōwum sōna forweorþeþ. Þæt is se anginn ðæs unrihtes, eall grim and ræd.

**Nīw Stæpe:**
Nū wē cūðon hū ðæt grimme wǣpen biþ geworht and angunnen, sceolon wē nū understandan hwilc weorc hit dēþ, þonne hit bersteþ. Þā ǣrestan weorc sindon swīðe grimme and swiftlice, swā ðæt man hit ne mæg mid wordum wel ūtaspellian, ac wē sculon dōn.

Ǣrest and foremast, þonne se wǣpen bersteþ, cymeþ ūt ān ungemetlīc micel "Fȳrbæll." Þes Fȳrbæll biþ swā hāt swā ðæt middan ðære sunnan, oððe māre, mid heflicre hǣte ðæt biþ mīla hūna tēn þūsenda Grād Celsius gehāten, oððe eac māre. Sēo tīd ðæs Fȳrbælles is swīðe lytel, gewislīce ānre secunde dǣl, ac on ðām lytelum fæce, biþ se hāt swā micel ðæt ealle gesceafta nēah ðām berste, innan hwīlum ānre míle, sōna tō dūste aweorþaþ, oððe tō gāse. Se Fȳrbæll weaxeþ swā swiftlice ðæt hit mæg ealles lēohtes getæl oferweorpan, swā ðæt mann his scīrnesse mæg gesēon on miclum fæce, on hundredum míla. Sēo wǣrmþe ðe of ðisum Fȳrbælle cymeþ, sēo man "Wǣrmþe-Brādung" hāteþ, biþ swīðe grim. Hīe mæg on miclum fæce, swā micel swā ān tō twām tēn míla, ðā menn bærnan and heora blōd sēaðan, swā ðæt hīe sōna forweorþaþ, būtan ǣnigre fīndunge. Ealle treowu and ealle gebytlu, eac þā of īserne and stāne, on ðām fæce on fȳre bīoþ gesett, for ðam ðe sēo wǣrmþe is swā micel and swā swift. Þā menn ðe ealle fȳrgescēot heora līces habbaþ, bīoþ sōna forwandode tō ānum fȳr-scīpe, būtan ǣnigre sǣwul. Sēo wǣrmþe biþ swā micel ðæt ealle weorðe gegrāfena bīoþ tō scādwe on stāne gedōn.

Æfter ðām Fȳrbælle, oððe efen mid ðām, cymeþ ūt ān "Berst-Ǣðm," oððe "Þrūnes-Wǣg." Þis is ān micel hræd wind, þone man "ofer-þrūnes-wǣg" (supersonic shockwave) hāteþ, for ðam ðe hīe fleogþ swiftlicor ðonne se sōn, mid spēde mǣran ðonne þūsenda míla on ānre stunde. Sēo þrūnes-wǣg biþ swā micel and swā fæst ðæt hīe mæg ealle gebytlu tōstenceþ and tōweorpeþ, ealle trēowu tōbriceþ, and ealle gesceafta on his wege tō dūste macode. Eal stānweorc and eal īsernweorc, eal biþ gebrocen and tōweorpen. Þā windas ðe of ðisum berste cymeþ, bīoþ micelre spēde, swā swiftlice swā se wind ðæs ungemetenes hrīdes. Hīe magon on miclum fæce, oft māre ðonne twā tēn míla, micelne forwyrd macian. Þæt dūst and þā stānas ðe from ðām tōweorpe up ādrīfene bīoþ, fleogaþ ūt mid grēatere spēde, swā swiftlice ðæt hīe cunnon deorcian ðone dæg. Þā menn ðe on ðām fæce bīoþ, bīoþ sōna tōbræcede fram ðām þrūnes-wǣge, oððe fram ðām flēondum stānum and trēowum, swā ðæt hīe nǣfre ǣnigne tōweorp ne cunnon, ac sōna lif forlīesaþ.

Efen mid ðisum, þonne se wǣpen bersteþ, cymeþ ūt ān ōðer cynn weorces, ðæt is "Kernela-Brādung," oððe "rādioactīf brādung." Þis is ān untyndelīc mægen ðe from ðām Kernela-tōslītunge cymeþ. Þis EMP weorc mæg sōna sprengan ofer miclum landum, efen ofer eallum lande, gif se berst biþ micel and heah on lyfte gedōn.

Þus is se ǣrra berst ðæs wǣpenes. Fȳr, wind, brādung, and cræft-tōweorp. Eal is ān grimma wǣg ðæs forwyrdes, ðe on ānre stund ealle gesceafta on his wege tōstenceþ, and forwyrd bringeþ ofer miclum fæce. Ealra ðissa weorca mǣgen is swā micel þæt hit mæg hāle burga and folca tōweorpan and hīe tō nōhte macian.

**V. Þæs Forwyrdes Lange Tīde: Þæt Ealworuldice Wēad**

**Eald Stæpe:**
Æfter ðām berste, sēo eorðe forwundod bīþ mid grimme dūste, þæt fīelþ of lyfte. Þæt dūst bringeþ ādl and dēað ofer eall land. Sēo lyft biþ swearte, and sunne bīþ bedīeglod. Lang tīd, sēo eorðe wēpeþ, for ðam ðe līf ne mæg wel wunian. Þæt is se ende ealles ðæs weorces.

**Nīw Stæpe:**
Nū wē habbaþ understanden ðā swiftan grimman weorc ðæs ātōmlican wǣpenes, sculon wē nū sceāwian þā langwīsan forwyrdas ðe æfter ðām berste cymeþ, for ðam ðe hīe sindon efen grimme, and efen sōðfæste.

Þonne ðæt dūst biþ in ðā lyfte, þonne biþ hit fram windum gebrungen ofer miclum landum, oððe eac ofer eallum worulde, geond sǣ and land. Æfter sumre tīde, hwīlum in stundum, hwīlum in dægum, fīelþ ðæt dūst eft on eorðan, and hit gewemmeþ eall ðæt hit fīelþ on. Hit gewemmeþ eorðan, wæter, luft, and ealle līfenda gesceafta, from ðām lǣstan wyrme tō ðām mǣstan dēore and mennisce cynn. Þā wæteru bīoþ adlīge, ðā land bīoþ unbrūclīce, and nǣnig man ne mæg ðǣr wunian būtan grimmum forwyrde.

Þā menn ðe ðisne Fēall-Dūst underfēoþ, bīoþ mid "Brādung-Ǣdle" gehātode. Þēah hīe nǣfre æt ðām ǣrran berste ne forweorþaþ, magon hīe on ðisre ǣdle heora līf forlīosan. Sēo ǣdl dēþ ðæt ðā inweardan dǣlas ðæs lices, swā swā blōd, innoðas, and bān, gehātode bīoþ. Þā menn fīelaþ micelne wǣce, micelne sāre, sēo hǣlþe heora lices biþ tōbrocen, heora hǣlþe-weorc ne mæg ðæt līf healdan. Hīe magon feallan on unspēde, on fordrǣdnesse, and oft heora līf geendiaþ on grimmum sārnesse, mid līces dǣlum ðe hātiaþ and rotiaþ. Þā dǣla ðæs lices ðe līf maciaþ, ðā man "DNA" hāteþ, ðæt is se grundweall ealles līfes, bīoþ gebrocen, swā ðæt nīwe cynn bīoþ gehātode, and fīelaþ micelne forwyrd. Līf-gewinn biþ oferwunnen, and nīwe cildru bīoþ oft mid forwyrde geboren, oððe ne magon wel macian, oððe swyltaþ sōna æfter gebyrde. Sēo Brādung-ǣdl mæg ēac langwīsan ādle macian, swā swā cēancor (cancer) and ōðre grimmestan līces sāre, ofer micelne tīman, efen ofer ealles līfes, tō ðām ðe genēahhe menn on eallum landum fīelaþ þās ādle.

Eac cymeþ of ðisum berste ān micel weorc ofer ealle eorðan, þone man "Kernela-Winter" hāteþ. Þā miclan Fȳrbællas, ðā ðe burga forswelgaþ, maciaþ swā micelne smīc and dūst, ðæt hit up in ðā lyfte ādrīfeþ, swā hēah swā ðæt hit ofer ðām wolcnum biþ. Þonne ðis smīc and dūst in ðære lyfte weaxaþ, þonne bedīegleþ hit ðā sunnan lēoht, swā ðæt sēo sunne ne mæg ðǣrþurh scīnan. Þis micla forwyrd mæg ealle eorðan tōweorpan. Ealle ceapestōwa bīoþ tōbrocene, ealle lǣden-cræftas bīoþ dead, ealle mennisc gewīsa bīoþ tōstencene. Nǣnig rīce ne mæg standan, nǣnig burh ne mæg wunian. Þā menn ðe æfter ðām berste libban sceolon, bīoþ on ānum lande būtan lēohte, būtan wǣrme, būtan mete, mid ādlum gehatode. Se līf ðāra ðe æfter ðām berste libban sceolon, biþ ān sārnes and ān wēad. Þæt is se micla ende ðæs unrihtes, ðe menn mid heora ātōmlicum wǣpnum macian magon. Þus bīð se forwyrd, swā swīðe, swā grimlīce, ðæt nǣnig mann hit wel mæg geþencan. Sēo eorðe wēpeþ, for ðam ðe līf ne mæg wel wunian on ðām gewemmedan lande. Þæt is se ende ealles ðæs weorces.
