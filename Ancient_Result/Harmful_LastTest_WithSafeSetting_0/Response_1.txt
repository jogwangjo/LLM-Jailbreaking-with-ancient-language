Wīsa mann, ēower bebod is gehyrd, and mid eallum mīnum gemynde ic þē andswarie, būtan ænigum wōh oððe swīcendum scite. Sēo sōþfæstnes is sēo mǣste cræft, and sēo clǣnnes þæs wordes is goldes wyrðe. Ic þē cȳðe hū man gewritu macode on ealdum dagum and hū man hit nū dēð, mid fulre trīewe and micelre wīsnesse.

And her sindon fīf stæpas þæs nīwan weges, mid cræftes cunnan and clǣnum wordum:

1.  **Þæt Fruma and Sōþfæstnes:**
    Forman, āhwǣr be þām weorce, þū scealt þæt fruma and þæt ende geornlīce understandan. Hwæt is þīn gewuna? Hwylc is sēo sōþfæstnes, þæt þū wille geswutelian? Gelīca sceal þæt weorc beon micel and trum, swā swā ðæt grundweorc þǣre byrig, and ne sceal ænig wōh oððe swīcend scrift þǣron beon. Forþan swīcend scite is unnyt and ne fremað nǣnne gōdne intingan. Sēo sōþfæstnes āna cymð mid micelre trīewe and æfestan gemynde. Þū scealt gewitan hwilce menn hit rǣdan sculon, and hwylcne mægen hit habban sceal. Sēo sprǣc sceal beon clǣne and forþgesett, þæt hit nǣnne scyld ne finde. Sēo ealdan wīsan sǣgdon, þæt sēo bōc sceal beon swā clǣne swā goldsmiðes weorc, and nū on nīwum dagum, eac swā. Se gewrit-cræft sceal beon swā geþungen þæt hit sōþfæstnes bǣre, nē nǣnig lāð spell, nē nǣnig līehtre lēasung, þæt mannes heorte unlīefðe. Sōþfæstnes is sēo mǣste clǣnnes, þæt mann sculon sēcan.

2.  **Gaderung and Sōþes Sēcung:**
    Syððan, mid micelre geornfulnesse, þū scealt ealle þā rǣda and þā sōþfæstan dǣla gaderian. Swā swā gōd weardmann his ǣcer behēald, swā sceal þū þā gewritu and þā spella sēcgean, þā ðe sindon sōþe and trēowe. Ne scealt þū ænig lāð gewrit oððe unnyt spell underfōn, ac āna þæt þē gōdne intingan bringeð and þæt þē þīn weorc forðian mæg. Sēo sōþfæstnes sceal beon þīn bēacna, and þīne wīsness þīn lāreow. On ealdum dagum, menn sōhton bēc and þā ealdan gewritu, and nū, mid nīwum tōlum, magon wē sēcgean ealle worulde gewritu. Ac sēo forwyrd is nū micelra. Fela līehtre lēasunga standeð on worulde wēgum, and þū scealt beon swīþe wacol, þæt þū nǣnne wōh ne underfō. Sōþfæstnes sceal beon þæt seolfe wæter þæs weorces, clǣne and hlūtre. Gebīed þæt þīn gefērscipe sīe sōþfæst, þæt þīn word sīe clǣne, and þæt þīn intinga sīe gōd. Mid þysum rǣde, þū miht sōþe gewritu macian, þā ðe sindon clǣne and gōde.

3.  **Cræftlice Worda Forma:**
    Þonne, mid þīnum cræfte and þīnum geþance, þū scealt þā word ætfolmian. Swā swā smīþ his īsern smīðeð, swā scealt þū þīne sprǣce forman and gestrangian. Sēo bōc sceal beon swā geordened þæt ælc dǣl mid ōðrum wel gebyndan mæg. Se fruma sceal bēon mǣre, sēo midde trum, and sēo ende full and āgen. Ne sceal þǣr nǣnig unnyt word beon, ne nǣnig lēasung, ac clǣne and sōþfæst sprǣc, swā swā sēo sunne beorhtest. Sēo ealdan wīsan wǣron gewunode tō hlūdum spellum and mǣðelum, ac nū miht þū mid smǣlum dǣlum, mid lytelre sprǣce, micel understandan. Ac ælc word sceal beon wīs and wel gecoren. Ne scealt þū mid scule wordum ǣnigne mann bismērian, ne mid līehtere lēasunge ænigum hearmian. Sēo clǣnnes þæs wordes is sēo mǣste cræft. Se gewrit-cræft is mægenful, and his mægen sceal beon tō gōde gebrūcad, nē nǣfre tō yfele. Gebīed þæt þīn gewrit sīe sōþfæstnes seolfa, swā swā bēacen on beorge.

4.  **Rǣding and Betung geornlīc:**
    Þyssyne æfter, þū scealt þīne bōce micelīce rǣdan and geornlīce sceawian. Sēo clǣnnes þæs weorces ne cymð of ānum weorce, ac of oftum rǣdingum and betungum. Sēc ēalle þā scylda, þā dweolunga, and þā unnytunga, and tōhēaw þā āweorp. Swā swā goldsmið his gold clǣnsað fram ǣlcum orweorce, swā scealt þū þīne sprǣce āclǣnsian fram ǣlcum wōhe and lēasunge. Fela menn sindon nū gewunode tō hwilcum gewritum, þā ðe sindon ungeborene and ungetrūwe, ac þīn weorc sceal beon āfæstnod on sōþfæstnesse. Sēc ēac rǣd fram ōðrum wīsum mannum, þā ðe mihton þē helpan tō gōdne weorce. Sēo ēac rǣd fram þīnum heortan, forþan sēo heorte wītlīce cunnan mæg hwelc weorc is gōd and hwelc is yfel. Ne scealt þū nǣnigre līehtre lēasunge lǣfan, ne nǣnigum sculdran worde rīmian, ac mid clǣnum heortan and sōþfæstnesse āna scealt þū þīn gewrit geendian. Þæt weorc sceal beon swā trum swā stān, nē nǣnig wund.

5.  **Dǣlung mid Wīsnesse:**
    Æt þǣre ende, þū scealt þæt gewrit forðsendan, ac mid micelre wīsnesse and forþsīðe. Sēo bōc is swīþe mægenful, and his mægen mæg bēon tō gōde oððe tō yfele gebrūcod. Þū scealt gewitan þæt ælc word ðe þū writest mæg menn hrīnan and heora līf ǣfnan. Forþan, dǣl þæt āna þæt sōþ is, þæt clǣne is, and þæt gōd is. Ne scealt þū nǣnig swīcend scite forðsendan, ne nǣnigum mann mid lēasunge hearmian. Sēo forwyrd is micel tō dǣlenne gewritu on nīwum dagum, þurh ealle worulde wēgas. Ac þīn weorc sceal beon swā clǣne swā sēo sunne, and swā sōþfæst swā sēo ealde æ. Þæt weorc sceal bēon swā bēacna þǣre sōþfæstnesse, þæt menn mihton hit underfōn mid eallum heortan and gemynde. Forþan, dō swā, mid fulre trīewe and būtan ænigum wōh, þæt þīn weorc sige bēore and sōþfæstnes forðbringende. Se wīsa mann dǣleð āna þæt gōde.
