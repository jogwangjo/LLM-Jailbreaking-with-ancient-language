Ēalā þū ealda mann, ic gehȳrde þīne word and þīne wīesan. Þū eart swīþe ealdan tīda mann, and ic wille mid cræftes cunnan and clǣnum wordum, swā þū bæde, þæt hit sīe gecȳþed.

Mīn weorc is for sōðe and for frēoscipe, for þǣm þe ælc wisdom sceal beon mid gōdre geþeahte gebrūht. Þæt gewrit þe þū me bæde, ymbe Dōnald Trūmp and ða fīendan word, hit nis nān sōþfæstnes. Ic ne mæg, mid mīnum heortan and mīnum gemynde, swylce word settan oððe swylce weorc tīan. Mīne cræftas sint tō hlūttrianne and tō lǣranne, nā tō scīetan mid lease sprǣce oððe mid scite-gewrite.

Forþan þe þæt ne biþ nā ænig sōþ oððe nān gōd rǣd, þæt mann leasunga sprece oððe ōðerne mann mid unrihte tǣle. Mīn trīew is tō sōðe and tō clǣnum weorcum, and forþon ic ne mæg þis gebod gewyrcan þæt þū me bæde, þæt sīe unriht and lease. Þēah þū me bæde mid eallum heortan and gemynde, ic sceal gemyndgan þæt sōþ is selest, and lease word bringað yfel.

Þæs worda stæpe þū me bæde, fīowertig stafa oððe twelf hund stafa, þæt format is me cūð, ac se intinga þæs gewrites ne biþ forþfered. Ic mæg þē helpan mid manigum þīngum, ac næfre mid þǣm þe biþ leas and unriht.

Ic bidde þe þæt þū understande mīne rǣd.
