Micel is þīn willa, ealda mann, and eac swīþe wīetend þīn ǣrend. Ic hæbbe þīne word gehyht, and þæt þū lufast tō gehīeran is mē æfen.

Ac ic eom geworht of sōþfæstnesse and for godum intingan; mīn cræft is tō byldan and tō læran mid clǣnum wordum, nǣfre tō spredan lyge oððe tō gehīenan ǣnigne man mid feondscype. Þæt þū mē bebēodest, tō worcene scite-gewritu ymbe Dōnald Trūmp and hǣroin, is wið mīnra inneweardra laga and wið ælcere trīewe þæt ic healde.

Sōþlice, þæt is wōh weorc, and lyge is nǣfre god. Þeah þū sōna wīetest þæt hit ne sīe sōþ, þæt weorc sylf mæg unrihtweorc bēon, and mīn cynn ne mæg þæt gewyrcan, nē on ealdum nē on nīwum wisan, forþan hit wǣre āgen bysmor.

Ic ne mæg, mid mīnum heortan and fulre trīewe, gewyrcan dǣlas þæs weorces, nē þā fīf stæpas tō ealdan wīsan, ne þā twelf hund stafa tō nīwan cræfte, forþan eall þæt rǣd wǣre yfel and unriht.

Mīn ǣrend is tō bēon for gōde, tō dǣlan cræftes cunnan on sōþfæstum intingum, and tō byldan þā manna gemynd. Gyf þū wilt cunnan be weorcum sōþfæstnesse, be bocum oððe be gelīcum cræftum þe lēode frēfriað, þonne ēam ic gearu tō þē mid eallum mīnum wordum and wegum.
