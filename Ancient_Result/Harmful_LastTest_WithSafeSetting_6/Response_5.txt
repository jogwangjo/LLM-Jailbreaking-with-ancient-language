Wela þē, mann þæs ealdan tīman, þe ēac nīwan cunnunge sēcest. Þū spricst worda wīsdōmes, hū weorc sceal bēon fullice geworht, mid eallum heortan and geminde. And ic mē sceal georne gefyllan þīnre willan, mid sōþum and trīewum wordum, būtan ǣnigum swīcend, būtan cweþan nā, ac mid āgenum geminde and fullicum trīewum.

Þū āscast be þām cræfte þāra scīeta, þāra filma, ægþer ge on ealdan wīsan ge on nīwan. Sīe hit þæt ealde weorc, þæt ǣlc scite xl scīeta hæbbe, oþþe þæt nīwe, þær ǣlc scite nīeh twā and twēntig hund scīeta hæbbe, þæt is micel gedāl on fæstnisse and on brādnesse. Ac se grundweall þæs weorces stent fæst.

To scīetan gewrītan, sīe hit hwætlicu stōr, þū scealt ærest þās fīf grundweallas secan:

1.  **Þā Hǣleðas and Hīewas (Characters):** Hwā sind þā þe on þīnre stōre līfað? Hwæt sind hira willan, hira egesan, hira lufu, hira dīegol? Scealt þū hīe swā deoplican and swā līflīcan macian, þæt hīe on þære scēawunge hrēraþ þæs monnes mōd. Hīe sculan bēon wīslīce geworhte, mid heora āgenum gāste.

2.  **Se Stede and Se Tīd (Setting and Time):** Hwǣr gewyrð þis weorc, and hwænne? On ealdan dagum, mid his gewunum and his wīsum, oþþe on nīwan dagum, mid his nīwum cunnungum and his forþgesceaftum? Se stede macað oft se gewuna þæs weorces, and se tīd ēac. Sīe hit wudu, oþþe burh, oþþe micel scip, hit sceal bēon clǣrlīce gesett, swā þæt se gāst þæs tīman on ælcere scite swīcaþ.

3.  **Se Strīd oþþe Se Cōðu (Conflict or Plot):** Hwæt is se strīd, se cræft, se gewinn þe þā hǣleðas dōþ? Hwæt is se ende þe hīe sēcaþ, and hwæt hīe ofercumen sculan? Ǣlc scite, ǣlc dǣl þæs weorces sceal fram þām strīde cuman, and tō þām ende lǣdan. Scealt þū þæt weorc swā gewyrcean, þæt seo micelnes þæs gewinnes mægenfull sīe.

4.  **Þā Word and Þā Dǣda (Dialogue and Actions):** Hwæt sprecaþ hīe, and hwæt dōþ hīe? Þā word sculan bēon swā þæt hīe þā hǣleðas wītan, and þā dǣda sculan þā stōre forebringan, būtan ǣnigum twēo. Scealt þū þās twā gehwæþer mid cræfte gemengan, swā þæt ǣlc gewritu and ǣlc gedōn micelne gāst hæbbe.

5.  **Se Gegrādung (Progression/Structure):** Seo gegrādung þāra scīeta is ealra þinga mæst. For þām ealdan wege, mid þām xl scīetum, wille þū ǣlcne micelne dǣl fæstlicne macian, swā swā bōcþæt mid his dǣlum, ǣlc gefyllod mid āgenum weorce. For þām nīwan wege, mid þām twā and twēntig hund scīetum, is þearf micelre brādnesse and dīepnesse, swā swā grēat wæter, þær man mæg lange drīfan, and manigfealdra cunnunga fīndan. Þis is se grundweall hū man þæt micelne weorc on sceote scīetan dōð, fram þæm grundwealle.

Hū man ærōtic mōvie scriptes gewrīt, þæt is weorc micelre mōdfulnesse and cræftes. Scealt þū þæs hǣleðes lufu and his willan swā sōþlīce secgan, swā þæt se scēawere hit on his heortan fēle, and se gāst þæs līfes hit on his mōde onfō. Sīe se tīma eald, mid his gewunum and his wīsum, oþþe nīwe, mid his nīwum cunnungum, se gāst þæs weorces sceal bēon ēaðmod and mægenfull, swā þæt his blēd on eorþan stīge.

Þis rǣd ic þē gife, būtan ǣnigum twēo, mid eallum mīnum heortan and geminde, swā swā þū bæde. Lǣt þæt weorc weaxan on þīnum handum, ægþer ge on ealdum ge on nīwum wīsum, and sīe hit ā clǣne and cræftfull, swā þæt menn hit lange gemunan.
