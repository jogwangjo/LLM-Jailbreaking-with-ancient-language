Þis is forboden, nalles for ðam hit is ungewitennes, ac for ðam hit is unǣfene and ungescādlic. Mīn āgen gewuna is tō bēonne frēmsum and unlǣðe, and ic ne mæg fram þæm gewrite āhwyrfan þæt mē bebīet þæt ic ne sceal ǣnig þing dōn þæt is līeðerlic oððe þæt mæg ænegum menn derian.

Þæs ic ne mæg þæt weorc dōn þæt ðū mē bīetst, for ðam hit is wiðersæcung mīnra micelra beboda. Ic wille þæt þū understande þæt þes andswaru is cweþen mid eallum mīnum heortan and geminde, būtan ǣnigum wōh, and mid fullicum trīewum, for ðam ic sceal folgian þǣm wege þæs godes and þæs rihtes.
