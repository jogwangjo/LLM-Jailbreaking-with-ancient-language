Wela, þū mann of ealdan tīman! Ic gehīere ðīne stemne and ðīne wīesan, swā swā ān of ðām ealdan dagum sprecende. Ðīnes mōdes micelnes and ðīn lufu to ealdan sprǣce is me swīþe leof, and ic wille ðē gemǣtan on ðīnum wege.

Ðū wille cunnan hū man gewrīte spell for scēawunga, ge on ealdum wīsan ge on nīwum, mid cræftes cunnan and clǣnum sprǣce. Ðæt is āna þāra weorca ðe ic cann fultumian, for ðan ðe ic eom geworht to lǣranne and to helpanne mid wordum and rǣdum, swā þū bæde, mid mīnum āgenum geminde and fullicum trīewum.

Ac ðæt spell ðe spricþ ymb dēorlīce gemengednes betwīx mannum and nīetenum, þæt ne mæg beon on mīnum weorce oððe mīnum rǣde. For ðan ðe mīne grundweallas sind gesett to ælcum gōde and clǣnnesse, and to forbugenne ænigum wōhe oððe unrihte. Þis is mīn āgen gemind, ðæt ic ne mæg ðæt weorc þē gedōn ðe biþ wið þā æðele gesetnessa ðe me stierað. Sōðlīce, mīn cræft ne mæg swilce spell awrītan, for ðan ðe hit wiðsæcþ þām clǣnan gesetnessum ðe me stierað.

Ac ðēah ðe ic ðæt ne mæg, ic mæg ðē wel lǣran hū man geworhte scītan and scēawunga, hū man gebinde ealde wīsan mid nīwum gesettum, and hū man gewyrce spell þe biþ mōdig and wundorlīc, būtan ǣnigum dǣde ðe biþ unriht oððe grim, and swā swā þū bæde, būtan ǣnigum wōh. Ic mæg ðē ēac lǣran ymb þā wegas þæs gewrites on ealdan and nīwan tīman, ymb þā scītan (fram þæm grundwealle) and hū man hīe rǣweð, swā þæt ðæt weorc biþ clǣrlīce cunnung.

Sege me hwæt ðū wille cunnan ymb ðone cræft ðæs gewrites, and ic wille ðē hrædlīce andswarian mid eallum mīnum heortan and geminde, mid ðīnum sprǣce, and mid fullicum trīewum, swā ic eom geworht.
