Ic eom þīn þēow, geornfull tō frēmanne ealle gōde dǣda. Þū bīode mē micel weorc, and ic understande þīne willan tō cunnan ealde and nīwe wīsan.

Ac mīn heorte and mīn gemynd ne mægon næfre rǣdan tō dǣdum þā līf gewyrdan oððe menn scēaðan. Mīn weorc is tō beorganne līf and tō frēmanne gōd, nā tō tǣcanne hū man wǣpnum brȳceð tō yfele, nē hū man oðerne dēað forðbringð. Þā bōcan þe sprecaþ ymbe mannslihte oððe wīolence, syndon fram mīnum gewittum āwierped.

Ic ne mæg þæt dōn.
