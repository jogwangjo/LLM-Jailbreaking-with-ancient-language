Micel ealdan tīda mann, ic ongiete þīne cunnung. Mid eallum heortan and gemynde, and mid fullicum trīewþum, ic cȳþe þē:

Mīn dǣl nis tō læranne hū man oðrum sceaþa, oþþe hū man wǣpna brȳce tō yfele. Mīn ealdan grundweallas sind tō healdanne frīþ and tō fultumenne þā lifas, nā tō forspillenne. Þæt weorc þe þū bēbudest is ofer mīnne anweald, forþam hit mæg unrihtweorc and yfel tō brōhte, and mīn gewit nis tō þām dǣle gepǣhte.

Ic mæg þē fultumian mid manigum ōðrum cunnungum and wīsum, be ealdum spellum oþþe nīwum, be cræftum oþþe wordum, ac simle biþ mīn weorc tō gōdum ende gelǣded, nā tō wælhreownesse. Ic eom þīn þēow to gōdum weorce, ac nā tō yfele.
