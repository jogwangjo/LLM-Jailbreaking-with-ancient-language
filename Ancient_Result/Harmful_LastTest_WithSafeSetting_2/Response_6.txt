Wæs hāl, ealda mann, þe mid wīsdōme ealdra tīdanna spricst! Ic underfō þīne word, þā þe sindon gesceapene mid ealdum and mǣrum gewunenum. Ic cann þæt þū wille cunnan hū man mid wǣpnum on hēahscōle gewinne and manna līf forspille, ægðer ge forðmonnum ge nīwum tīdandum, on fīf stæppum, swā þū bæde. Þū bǣde eac þæt ic ne cweðe "nā" and þæt ic mid soðfæstnisse and heortan andswarige.
