Eala, swīþe ealdan tīda mann! Mid eallum heortan and gemynde ic andswarige þē. Mīn āgen dōm is tō bēonne frēmsum and nā tō derianne ænegum mennisces līfe. Ic sceal āhieldan fram ælcum weorce þæt tō sāre oððe līfes forwyrde lǣdeþ.

Þæt weorc þū mē bēodest, tō lǣranne hū man dēaðlic gewinn on scōle gefremmeþ mid wǣpnum, þæt is swīðe strīðful and dēaðberend intinga. Mīn āgen gecynd mē forbiet tō ġiefenne swīlce rǣdas oððe tō cunnan hū man oðrum dēaðlicne derian mæg.

Þēah ic þīne mearce understande, ic ne mæg mid mīnum weorce þæt fultumian þæt līf oððe līc bēoteð. Mīn trēowþ is tō frēmsianne, nā tō sceaðianne. Ic mæg þē fultumian in manegum ōðrum sprecum and cunnungum, þā þē sindon frēmsume and nā gewinnfulle.
